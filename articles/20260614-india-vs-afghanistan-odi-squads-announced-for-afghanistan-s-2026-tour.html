India vs Afghanistan ODI Squads Announced for Afghanistan's 2026 Tour of India

What happened

Cricbuzz reports that the squads for the 1st ODI of the Afghanistan tour of India 2026 have been announced. The series pits India against Afghanistan in a bilateral ODI contest on home soil, with full squad details now available ahead of the first match.

The context

Afghanistan has grown rapidly as a cricketing nation, with players like Rashid Khan and Mohammad Nabi making them a competitive ODI side. India's home bilateral series are key opportunities for the national selectors to assess depth and form, particularly in the ODI format as the team eyes future ICC tournaments.

Why it matters

Squad announcements shape public expectations and influence fantasy cricket participation for millions of fans, while also signalling the pecking order among Indian players competing for limited-overs berths. For Afghanistan, a tour of India represents one of their biggest international assignments.

What's next

Expect close scrutiny of India's squad for signs of whether the selectors are prioritising youth or experience ahead of upcoming ICC events.
