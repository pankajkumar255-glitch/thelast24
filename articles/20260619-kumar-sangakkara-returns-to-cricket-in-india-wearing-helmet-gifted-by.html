Kumar Sangakkara returns to cricket in India wearing helmet gifted by Yashasvi Jaiswal

Key facts

  • Kumar Sangakkara returned to cricket in India wearing Yashasvi Jaiswal's gifted helmet
  • The gesture went viral on social media
  • Sangakkara is Director of Cricket at Rajasthan Royals; Jaiswal plays for the same franchise

News18 reports that cricket legend Kumar Sangakkara has returned to playing cricket in India, and the moment that caught everyone's attention was him wearing a helmet gifted by India's young opening batter Yashasvi Jaiswal. The gesture went viral on social media, with fans across the cricket world reacting warmly to the cross-generational exchange.

Sangakkara, widely regarded as one of the greatest batters in cricket history and currently serving as the Director of Cricket at Rajasthan Royals in the IPL, was seen sporting the Jaiswal-gifted headgear during his return to the crease. Jaiswal, who plays for Rajasthan Royals in the IPL, has been working closely with Sangakkara in a mentor-student dynamic.

For Indian cricket fans, the image of a legend like Sangakkara choosing to wear Jaiswal's helmet is a powerful endorsement of the 22-year-old's standing in world cricket. It also reflects the warm culture of respect within the sport and Jaiswal's rapidly growing reputation beyond India's borders.
