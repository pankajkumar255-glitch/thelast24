Focus stays on Rohit Sharma in second India-Afghanistan ODI

What happened

Hindustan Times reports that focus remains firmly on Rohit Sharma heading into the second India versus Afghanistan ODI. The piece highlights ongoing scrutiny of the India captain's batting form and his place in the team setup.

The context

Rohit Sharma has faced growing scrutiny over his form across formats in recent months. Afghanistan, ranked lower than India, represent a series where India are heavy favourites, making it an opportunity for senior players to put scores on the board and silence critics.

Why it matters

Rohit's performance directly influences selection decisions ahead of upcoming high-stakes tournaments, making this series consequential despite the modest opposition. Fans and analysts are watching to see whether he can recapture the consistency that made him one of India's greatest ODI batsmen.

What's next

Expect the spotlight to intensify on Rohit's innings in the second ODI, with any significant score or failure likely to fuel fresh debate about his international future.
