Drishyam 3 crosses Rs 330 crore worldwide, OTT release date announced

What happened

The Indian Express reports that Drishyam 3 has crossed Rs 330 crore in worldwide box-office collections and that an OTT release date has been officially announced. The film continues the acclaimed Drishyam franchise and has been performing strongly at the box office since its release.

The context

The Drishyam series, directed by Jeethu Joseph and starring Mohanlal, became a landmark franchise in Indian cinema after the first film's enormous success led to a Hindi remake and sequels. Drishyam 2 was a major OTT and theatrical success, raising expectations sharply for the third instalment.

Why it matters

A Rs 330 crore worldwide run confirms Malayalam cinema's sustained power at the box office and signals strong OTT viewership numbers ahead. For fans, the OTT announcement means they can now plan to watch it at home.

What's next

Expect the OTT platform hosting Drishyam 3 to announce a specific streaming date shortly, likely driving a significant spike in subscriptions.
