Haunted 2 earns Rs 2.5 cr, outperforms both Imtiaz Ali and Kangana Ranaut releases

What happened

The Indian Express reports that Haunted 2 earned Rs 2.5 crore, surpassing both Main Vaapas Aaunga — directed by Imtiaz Ali — and Bharat Bhhagya Viddhaata starring Kangana Ranaut in their respective box office collections during the same period.

The context

Imtiaz Ali is one of Hindi cinema's most respected directors, known for films like Jab We Met and Tamasha. Kangana Ranaut is a National Award-winning actress with a high media profile. The fact that a modestly produced horror film has outearned both releases reflects a wider trend where audience choices are driven by genre preference and word-of-mouth rather than marquee names alone.

Why it matters

For producers and studio heads, this signals a need to recalibrate investment in high-cost, star-driven projects when genre films can deliver comparable or better returns at a fraction of the budget. For audiences, it suggests greater variety at cinemas as genre films gain commercial credibility.

What's next

Expect trade analysts and film financiers to closely study Haunted 2's full week run to determine whether it can sustain its lead and whether horror as a genre warrants larger production budgets going forward.
