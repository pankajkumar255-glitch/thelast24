Deepti Sharma and Smriti Mandhana star as India beat Pakistan at Women's T20 World Cup 2026

What happened

BBC and olympics.com report that India Women beat Pakistan Women in the Women's T20 World Cup 2026, with Smriti Mandhana and Deepti Sharma starring in the victory. India Today adds that Deepti produced another ICC event masterclass, with the all-rounder credited as a pivotal figure in the team's performance. The win was described as an easy one, suggesting a comfortable margin.

The context

India vs Pakistan in any ICC event is one of cricket's most-watched contests, drawing massive viewership across the subcontinent. Deepti Sharma has steadily emerged as one of the world's best women's all-rounders, contributing crucial runs and wickets in pressure situations. Smriti Mandhana is India's premier batter in the shortest format and regularly sets the tone at the top of the order.

Why it matters

A win over Pakistan boosts India's net run rate and confidence heading deeper into the tournament, making their path to the knockout stages clearer. For millions of Indian cricket fans, a victory in this fixture carries emotional weight well beyond the points table.

What's next

Expect India Women to carry this momentum into their next group-stage fixture, with Deepti Sharma likely to remain the key player opposition teams plan around.
