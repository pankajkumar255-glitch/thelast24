Drishyam 3 leads a packed week of new OTT releases from June 15–21

What happened

The Times of India reports that the week of June 15–21 brings a strong lineup of new OTT releases, led by Drishyam 3, the third film in the hit Drishyam franchise, along with international title Your Fault: London and the second season of Indian series Thukra Ke Mera Pyaar, among other titles dropping across platforms.

The context

The Drishyam franchise, based on the Malayalam original and remade in multiple Indian languages, is one of the most commercially successful thriller series in Indian cinema. Its previous instalments were massive hits both theatrically and on streaming. The growing trend of franchise sequels premiering directly on OTT platforms reflects the maturing of India's streaming market, which now competes directly with multiplexes for premium content.

Why it matters

A direct-to-OTT release for a franchise as large as Drishyam signals growing confidence in streaming as a primary destination for big-ticket Indian entertainment, potentially reshaping how future sequels are distributed. For viewers, it means premium content at home without theatre pricing.

What's next

Expect viewership numbers for Drishyam 3 on its streaming platform to be closely watched as a barometer of whether major franchise films can drive subscriber growth on Indian OTT.
