South Indian OTT Roundup: From Blast to Baahubali: The Torchbearer, Here's What's Streaming This Week

Key facts

  • Baahubali: The Torchbearer is among this week's South Indian OTT releases
  • Blast is also among the new arrivals
  • Releases span Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada content
  • New titles available from June 26–28

This week's OTT calendar for South Indian language content is headlined by Baahubali: The Torchbearer, a new addition to the franchise that S.S. Rajamouli's original films turned into one of India's most recognisable cinematic brands. The title joins Blast and a clutch of other new Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada titles arriving on streaming platforms between June 26 and June 28.

The Baahubali franchise carries enormous commercial and cultural weight in India, and any new content bearing that name is likely to draw immediate viewership across language communities. Whether The Torchbearer is a film, series, or animated project, its release on OTT represents the continued effort to extend proven IP into the streaming space rather than relying solely on theatrical runs.

The broader slate of South Indian releases underscores how productive the regional film industries have become as content suppliers for digital platforms. Malayalam cinema in particular has developed a reputation for quality originals on streaming, while Telugu and Tamil titles continue to draw pan-India audiences — a dynamic that has fundamentally shifted the power balance between Bollywood and the South.

For viewers, the week represents a strong window of new content across genres and languages. Platforms competing for South Indian audiences are increasingly treating these release windows as key engagement moments, especially as theatrical windows shorten and more productions go directly or quickly to streaming.
