Drishyam 3 leads a strong slate of South OTT releases this week

What happened

Moneycontrol reports that Drishyam 3 and Athiradi are among the notable South Indian OTT releases of the current week, highlighting a strong pipeline of regional content arriving on streaming platforms. The report lists multiple titles across languages as part of this week's South OTT slate.

The context

The Drishyam franchise, starring Mohanlal in the Malayalam original, is one of the most commercially successful thriller series in Indian cinema history, with remakes made in multiple languages including Hindi, Tamil, and Telugu. South Indian content has increasingly driven subscriber growth and engagement for major OTT platforms including Amazon Prime Video, Netflix, and Disney+ Hotstar. The franchise's OTT releases routinely rank among the most-viewed titles nationally.

Why it matters

For OTT platforms, a marquee title like Drishyam 3 is a significant traffic driver that can boost subscriptions and engagement metrics. For viewers, it means a high-profile, family-friendly thriller is available at home, intensifying competition for eyeballs across streaming services this week.

What's next

Expect Drishyam 3's streaming numbers and audience response to set the tone for how the franchise's next steps — including potential further sequels or spin-offs — are planned.
