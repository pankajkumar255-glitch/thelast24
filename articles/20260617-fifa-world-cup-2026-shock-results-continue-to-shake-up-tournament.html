FIFA World Cup 2026: Shock Results Continue to Shake Up Tournament

What happened

ESPN India reports that the FIFA World Cup 2026 saw shock draws on Monday, June 16, with live updates tracking surprising results across multiple matches. The outcomes have significantly shaken up group-stage standings.

Unexpected results affect fantasy leagues, fan sentiments, and the intense watercooler conversations that the World Cup generates across India, where football has a massive and growing following. Shock draws also reshape the group standings and knockout-stage prospects for fan-favourite teams.
