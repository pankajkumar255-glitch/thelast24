PM Modi's Sidebar With Trump At G7 As Indians Are Fired Upon In Iran

What happened

NDTV reports that Prime Minister Modi held a sidebar meeting with Donald Trump at the G7 summit in France amid reports of firing on Indians inside Iran. The report does not specify the number of casualties or the precise circumstances of the incident.

The context

India has a large diaspora and workforce across the Gulf and West Asia, including Iran. Modi has cultivated ties with both the US and Iran, making the simultaneous G7 diplomacy and the Iran safety crisis a test of India's multi-alignment foreign policy.

Why it matters

The safety of Indians in Iran is an immediate concern for thousands of families back home, while the Modi-Trump bilateral conversation at G7 could shape India's positioning on the new US-Iran nuclear deal and related sanctions.

What's next

Expect the Ministry of External Affairs to issue a statement on the welfare of Indians in Iran and details of what Modi and Trump discussed bilaterally.
