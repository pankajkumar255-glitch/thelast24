Shubman Gill reveals what Rohit Sharma told him after costly run-out vs Afghanistan

What happened

NDTV Sports reports that Shubman Gill disclosed what Rohit Sharma told him after he was run out during the first ODI against Afghanistan. The conversation apparently took place in the dressing room following the dismissal. Gill sharing the exchange publicly indicates the open communication that the Indian team management encourages.

The context

Shubman Gill is one of India's most important batting talents and has been groomed as a future leader of the side. Run-outs are costly errors in limited-overs cricket, and how captains and coaches respond to such mistakes has a direct impact on player confidence. Rohit Sharma is widely regarded as a supportive captain who prioritises player wellbeing alongside results.

Why it matters

Gill's continued form and confidence are critical to India's batting stability across all formats; supportive leadership helps ensure he is not derailed by individual errors. This glimpse into team dynamics also reassures fans that the dressing room atmosphere under Rohit and Gambhir remains constructive.

What's next

Expect Gill to be keen to respond with a strong performance in subsequent ODIs against Afghanistan to put the run-out behind him.
