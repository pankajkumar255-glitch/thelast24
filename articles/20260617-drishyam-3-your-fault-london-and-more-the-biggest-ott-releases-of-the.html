Drishyam 3, Your Fault: London and more: The biggest OTT releases of the week (June 15–21)

What happened

The Times of India reports that the OTT releases for the week of June 15–21 include Drishyam 3, Your Fault: London, and Thukra Ke Mera Pyaar 2, among others. The lineup covers Hindi, South Indian and international content across major streaming platforms.

The context

The Drishyam franchise, starring Ajay Devgn in the Hindi version and Mohanlal in the original Malayalam series, has been a consistent blockbuster property. Drishyam 2 (2022) was one of the biggest box-office hits of its year, making the third instalment one of the most eagerly awaited OTT premieres of 2025. OTT platforms have become the primary destination for franchise content reaching audiences who missed theatrical runs.

Why it matters

A strong week of OTT releases drives subscriber retention and new sign-ups for streaming platforms, while giving viewers compelling reasons to stay home — directly affecting multiplex footfall and weekend entertainment spending.

What's next

Expect Drishyam 3 viewership numbers to be closely watched as a benchmark for how franchise sequels perform on OTT versus theatrical release.
