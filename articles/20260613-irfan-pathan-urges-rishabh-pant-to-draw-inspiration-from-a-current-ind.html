Irfan Pathan urges Rishabh Pant to draw inspiration from a current India star

What happened

NDTV Sports reports that Irfan Pathan has advised Rishabh Pant to take inspiration from a current India cricket star, though the specific player named is referenced in the headline without full detail. Pathan, a respected voice in Indian cricket as a former player and current commentator, has been vocal about Pant's need to refine certain aspects of his game. The advice comes at a time when Pant is re-establishing himself in all three formats following his return from injury.

The context

Rishabh Pant suffered a serious road accident in December 2022 and was out of cricket for over a year, undergoing multiple surgeries before making a triumphant comeback. He has since returned to international cricket and the IPL, but debates continue about whether he has fully recaptured his pre-accident form and whether his aggressive style needs refinement at the highest level. Irfan Pathan played 29 Tests and 120 ODIs for India and is now a prominent cricket analyst and commentator. Pant is widely regarded as one of India's most naturally gifted batters and a match-winner in all conditions.

Why it matters

Pant's development as a consistent match-winner is critical for India's batting depth across all three formats, particularly as senior players like Virat Kohli and Rohit Sharma move toward the end of their careers. Public advice from respected former players like Pathan shapes fan expectations and can influence the broader narrative around a player's form and selection. How Pant responds to such scrutiny will be closely watched over the coming months of international and IPL cricket.

What's next

Expect Pant's performances in the India-Afghanistan ODI series and the upcoming IPL season to be assessed against the standards that analysts like Pathan are publicly setting for him. His ability to blend aggression with greater consistency will be the key metric by which his progress is judged.
