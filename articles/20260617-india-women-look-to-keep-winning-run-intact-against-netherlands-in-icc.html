India women look to keep winning run intact against Netherlands in ICC Women's T20 World Cup 2026

What happened

Business Standard reports that India will take on the Netherlands in an ICC Women's T20 World Cup 2026 fixture, with the Indian team intent on maintaining their winning momentum in the tournament.

The context

The ICC Women's T20 World Cup is one of the most-watched events in women's cricket globally. India have consistently been contenders but are yet to win the title, making each tournament a fresh opportunity. The Netherlands are a lower-ranked side, making this a must-win fixture for the top teams.

Why it matters

A continued winning run boosts India's net run rate and confidence ahead of tougher knockout stage opponents, and raises the excitement levels for millions of fans following the women's team. Strong performances also help build the long-term commercial and competitive case for women's cricket in India.

What's next

Expect India to field a strong lineup against the Netherlands, with the focus on not just winning but building form and combinations ahead of the knockout rounds.
