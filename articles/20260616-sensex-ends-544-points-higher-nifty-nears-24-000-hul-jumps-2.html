Sensex ends 544 points higher, Nifty nears 24,000; HUL jumps 2%

What happened

India Today reports that the Sensex ended the session 544 points higher, while the Nifty 50 index moved close to the 24,000 level. HUL was a standout performer, gaining 2% on the day. The rally was broad-based across sectors.

The context

Indian markets have been navigating a mix of global signals — including US-Iran tensions, shifting trade routes, and domestic macro data. The Nifty 50 at 24,000 is a watched resistance level; a sustained break above it could trigger further institutional buying. HUL, as a bellwether FMCG stock, often reflects broader consumer sentiment and liquidity conditions.

Why it matters

Rising indices directly benefit the tens of millions of Indians invested in equity mutual funds through SIPs, while a stronger market also boosts corporate fundraising and economic confidence. HUL's outperformance signals optimism about consumer demand recovery.

What's next

Expect traders to watch whether the Nifty can decisively close above 24,000 in the next few sessions, which would signal a broader bullish trend.
