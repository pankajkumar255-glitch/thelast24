Cocktail 2 grosses Rs 76.25 crore globally in three days at box office

Key facts

  • Cocktail 2 collected Rs 76.25 crore globally in first 3 days
  • Film stars Shahid Kapoor
  • Day 3 live update reported by The Indian Express
  • Strong opening weekend sustains commercial momentum

Shahid Kapoor's Cocktail 2 has crossed Rs 76.25 crore globally in its first three days at the box office, according to live collection data reported by The Indian Express, marking a strong start for the much-anticipated sequel. The Day 3 figures confirm the film has held up well through the weekend, a crucial indicator of its overall commercial prospects.

The Rs 76-crore global number in three days positions Cocktail 2 comfortably among the better-performing Bollywood releases of the year so far. Global collections include both domestic India earnings and overseas box office, and the figure suggests the film has found an audience both at home and among the Indian diaspora abroad.

Shahid Kapoor's box office standing has been variable in recent years, with some films underperforming relative to expectations. A strong opening for Cocktail 2 will therefore be received as a personal vindication as much as a commercial success, reinforcing his marketability as a leading man in big-budget Hindi cinema.

With the first weekend now behind it, the film's weekday performance will be closely watched to determine its ultimate theatrical run. A strong word-of-mouth and sustained footfall through the working week would put Cocktail 2 on course for a significant overall collection. Trade analysts will update projections as the Day 4 and Day 5 numbers come in.
