Shreyas Iyer's man-management skills draw praise as T20 captaincy race heats up

Key facts

  • Shreyas Iyer praised for man-management in T20 captaincy race
  • Iyer captained KKR to 2024 IPL title
  • India's T20 captaincy is open amid post-Rohit transition
  • No formal appointment announced yet

The Times of India reports that Shreyas Iyer has earned notable praise for his man-management skills, positioning him as a prominent candidate in the ongoing race to become India's next T20 international captain. While no formal announcement has been made, the report highlights that Iyer's ability to handle players and foster dressing-room cohesion is being viewed favourably by those involved in the selection process.

Iyer has captained the Kolkata Knight Riders in the IPL and led them to the 2024 IPL title, giving him credible leadership credentials at the highest domestic level. His T20 batting record for India is strong, and the combination of on-field impact and off-field temperament appears to be resonating with selectors and team management.

With Rohit Sharma stepping back from Test cricket and India in a clear transitional phase, the T20 captaincy is one of the most significant appointments in Indian cricket right now. For fans, the choice will define the style and direction of the team heading into the next T20 World Cup cycle.
