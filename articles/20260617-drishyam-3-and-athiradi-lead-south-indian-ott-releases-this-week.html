Drishyam 3 and Athiradi Lead South Indian OTT Releases This Week

Key facts

  • Drishyam 3, third instalment of the Malayalam thriller franchise, now streaming
  • Tamil film Athiradi also among the week's OTT releases
  • Drishyam franchise stars Mohanlal as Georgekutty

Moneycontrol reports that this week's South Indian OTT slate is headlined by Drishyam 3, the third chapter in the landmark Malayalam thriller franchise, along with Tamil action film Athiradi and other regional releases now available for home streaming.

Drishyam 3 is one of the most closely watched OTT arrivals in recent memory. The franchise, featuring Mohanlal as Georgekutty, built a massive following across India through its first two instalments and set record numbers at the Kerala box office. The third film's digital release opens it up to the large number of viewers who follow the series on streaming platforms rather than theatres.

Athiradi is among the Tamil titles arriving alongside Drishyam 3, adding to a week that offers South Indian audiences a strong menu across Malayalam and Tamil languages. Other Kannada and Telugu titles are also part of the week's new OTT additions, as South Indian studios continue to feed a robust appetite for regional content on streaming services.

For OTT subscribers across India, the arrival of Drishyam 3 in particular marks a major streaming event — the culmination of a franchise that has been one of Indian cinema's most consistent crowd-pleasers over the past decade.
