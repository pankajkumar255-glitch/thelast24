Smriti Mandhana is the only Indian on TIME's 100 Most Influential People in Sports 2026

What happened

Business Standard, The Times of India, India Today, The Indian Express, and olympics.com all report that Smriti Mandhana has been named in TIME's 100 Most Influential People in Sports 2026 list. She is the only Indian to feature on the list. The announcement has drawn wide attention across Indian sports media.

The context

Smriti Mandhana is India's premier women's batter and one of the most recognised faces of women's cricket globally. TIME's annual influential sportspersons list spans athletes across all disciplines worldwide, making an Indian cricket inclusion — let alone a sole one — a significant distinction. Women's cricket in India has seen a surge in viewership and investment since the Women's Premier League was launched.

Why it matters

The recognition elevates the profile of Indian women's cricket internationally and could attract greater sponsorship and media attention to the sport domestically. For fans, it is a moment of national pride, placing an Indian woman athlete alongside the world's biggest sporting names.

What's next

Expect Mandhana's commercial profile and brand endorsements to strengthen further following this global recognition.
