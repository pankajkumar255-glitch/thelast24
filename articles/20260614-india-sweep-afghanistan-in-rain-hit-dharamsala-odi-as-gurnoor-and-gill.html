India sweep Afghanistan in rain-hit Dharamsala ODI as Gurnoor and Gill shine

What happened

India Today reports that India swept Afghanistan in the rain-hit 1st ODI at Dharamsala, with Gurnoor Brar impressing with the ball and Shubman Gill delivering a composed innings with the bat. ESPNcricinfo's match report confirms India's comprehensive victory in the series opener.

The context

India and Afghanistan have played a growing number of ODI contests in recent years, with India consistently the stronger side. Dharamsala's high-altitude pitch is known to assist fast bowlers early on, making Gurnoor's performance particularly noteworthy as he competes for a permanent spot in the Indian ODI setup.

Why it matters

The win keeps India's ODI form on track ahead of a packed international calendar, and Gurnoor Brar's breakout performance could signal a new pace option for selectors to consider. Gill's fluent batting continues to cement his status as India's premier white-ball opener.

What's next

Expect the 2nd ODI of the India-Afghanistan series to be closely watched as selectors finalise combinations for upcoming high-stakes assignments.
