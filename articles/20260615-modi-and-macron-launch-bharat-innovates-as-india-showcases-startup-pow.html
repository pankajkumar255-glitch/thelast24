Modi and Macron Launch 'Bharat Innovates' as India Showcases Startup Power on Global Stage

What happened

Outlook Business reports that Prime Minister Narendra Modi and French President Emmanuel Macron launched 'Bharat Innovates', an initiative designed to showcase India's startup ecosystem on a global platform. The launch placed India's entrepreneurial strength at the centre of a high-profile bilateral engagement between the two countries.

The context

India is home to over 100 unicorns and one of the world's third-largest startup ecosystems. The Modi government has consistently used international platforms to attract foreign investment into Indian technology and innovation. France under Macron has been an active partner in deepening tech and strategic ties with India.

Why it matters

A joint initiative with a G7 leader lends India's startup story significant global credibility, potentially attracting European venture capital and partnerships for Indian entrepreneurs. It also strengthens the bilateral relationship at a time when India is diversifying its strategic and economic alliances.

What's next

Expect specific collaboration announcements and investment commitments tied to Bharat Innovates to emerge from follow-up bilateral meetings between Indian and French officials.
