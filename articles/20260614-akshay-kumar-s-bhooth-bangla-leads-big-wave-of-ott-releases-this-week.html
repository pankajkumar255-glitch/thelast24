Akshay Kumar's Bhooth Bangla Leads Big Wave of OTT Releases This Week

What happened

The Times of India and The Indian Express both report a strong OTT release lineup for the week of June 8–14, with Akshay Kumar's Bhooth Bangla, Ali Fazal's crime series Raakh, Jason Statham's Shelter, and Sweet Magnolias among the headline arrivals across Indian and global streaming services.

The context

Akshay Kumar has been among Bollywood's most prolific OTT contributors in recent years, with several of his productions going directly or quickly to streaming. The simultaneous arrival of multiple high-profile titles reflects the increasingly competitive Indian streaming landscape, where platforms vie for viewer attention each weekend.

Why it matters

A packed OTT slate means subscribers get strong value this week, but it also intensifies competition for viewership between Indian and international content on the same platforms. How Bhooth Bangla performs on streaming will be a key indicator of Akshay Kumar's digital audience pull.

What's next

Expect viewership rankings and early social media reactions for Bhooth Bangla and Raakh to emerge by mid-week, giving a first read on which titles are breaking through.
