Legends League Cricket 2026: Squads Out for MS vs INDCAP Opening Match

Key facts

  • Squads announced for MS vs INDCAP, 1st match of Legends League Cricket 2026
  • MS franchise is associated with former India captain MS Dhoni
  • Legends League Cricket features retired international cricketers

Cricbuzz reports that the squads for the first match of Legends League Cricket 2026 — between MS and INDCAP — have been officially announced. The Legends League Cricket is a tournament that brings retired international cricketers back to competitive play, and its season opener traditionally generates significant interest among fans nostalgic for past cricketing heroes.

The MS franchise draws particular attention given its association with former India captain MS Dhoni, one of the most popular figures in Indian cricket history. INDCAP, another franchise in the league, features its own complement of former internationals, and the opening match serves as a curtain-raiser for the wider Legends League season.

Legends League Cricket has grown in popularity since its inception, offering former stars a platform to remain active in competitive cricket while entertaining fans who may no longer see these players in mainstream T20 leagues. The league has previously featured veterans from India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, and beyond.

For everyday Indian fans, the league represents an opportunity to watch beloved retired cricketers in action once more, combining nostalgia with competitive sport in a format designed to keep veteran players engaged and visible.
