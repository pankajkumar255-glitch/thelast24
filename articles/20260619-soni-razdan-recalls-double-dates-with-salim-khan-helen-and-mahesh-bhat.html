Soni Razdan recalls double dates with Salim Khan, Helen, and Mahesh Bhatt in Bollywood's golden era

Key facts

  • Soni Razdan says she and Mahesh Bhatt double-dated with Helen and Salim Khan
  • Salim Khan is the co-writer of Sholay and Deewaar
  • Helen is one of Hindi cinema's most iconic dancer-actresses
  • Soni Razdan is Mahesh Bhatt's wife and Alia Bhatt's mother

The Indian Express reports that actress Soni Razdan has shared a candid personal memory about Bollywood's social circles from an earlier era, revealing that she and Mahesh Bhatt used to go on double dates with Helen and Salim Khan. The recollection paints a warm picture of camaraderie between some of Hindi cinema's most storied personalities.

Salim Khan is one half of the legendary Salim-Javed screenwriting duo, responsible for defining blockbusters like Sholay and Deewaar, while Helen is one of Hindi cinema's most iconic dancers and actresses. Mahesh Bhatt is a celebrated filmmaker known for films like Arth and Saaransh, and Soni Razdan is both his wife and a respected actress in her own right — the couple are also parents to Bollywood star Alia Bhatt.

The anecdote is a reminder of how closely knit Bollywood's inner world has always been, with personal friendships cutting across the industry's biggest talents. For fans of classic Hindi cinema, such glimpses into the private lives of its legends carry a nostalgic and human appeal that goes well beyond the screen.
