Drishyam 3 leads a packed week of South Indian OTT releases alongside Kenatha Kanom and Athiradi

What happened

The Economic Times and Moneycontrol both report that this week's South Indian OTT slate is headlined by Drishyam 3, with other releases including Kenatha Kanom and Athiradi across Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu and Kannada platforms. The releases span multiple streaming services and languages. Drishyam 3 is the latest chapter in Mohanlal's landmark Malayalam thriller franchise.

The context

The Drishyam franchise, starring Mohanlal and directed by Jeethu Joseph, is one of Malayalam cinema's most commercially successful series, with both earlier films — Drishyam (2013) and Drishyam 2 (2021) — becoming pan-India hits and spawning remakes in multiple languages. South Indian OTT content has seen explosive growth in post-pandemic India, with platforms competing aggressively for regional language titles. Weekly OTT release roundups have become a major driver of subscription renewals.

Why it matters

Drishyam 3's OTT arrival gives the tens of millions of fans across India who watched the first two films direct streaming access, potentially making it one of the most-watched OTT events of the month. It also signals the franchise's continued health as a premium South Indian IP.

What's next

Expect Drishyam 3's OTT viewership numbers to be closely watched by streaming platforms as a barometer of appetite for established South Indian franchise content.
