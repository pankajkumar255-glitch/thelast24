Vaibhav Sooryavanshi set to shine in India vs Ireland 1st T20I — here's when and where to watch

Key facts

  • India vs Ireland 1st T20I is the series opener
  • Vaibhav Sooryavanshi is the headline attraction for India
  • BBC says Sooryavanshi has the 'mental capabilities' to shine
  • Live streaming details confirmed by The Indian Express

India's blockbuster T20I series against Ireland gets underway with the first match firmly in the spotlight, thanks in large part to the anticipated appearance of Vaibhav Sooryavanshi, the teenager who has set Indian cricket ablaze with his batting exploits in recent months.

Sooryavanshi, who shot to national fame with his extraordinary performances at the IPL and domestic level, is expected to feature prominently and has been cited as a key attraction for viewers. The BBC reported ahead of the match that the youngster has the 'mental capabilities' to shine at international level, a ringing endorsement from observers watching his progress closely.

The Ireland series represents a calculated opportunity for India's selectors to blood young talent and test squad depth in a lower-pressure international environment ahead of bigger tournaments. Ireland, though not among the top-ranked T20 sides, have shown in recent years they are capable of springing surprises, and this tour will serve as genuine competitive preparation.

For fans, The Indian Express has confirmed live streaming options so viewers across India and beyond can follow Sooryavanshi's every move. With the series schedule and squads also confirmed by The Times of India, everything is in place for what promises to be a closely watched T20 assignment.
