Paras Defence, MTAR Tech, HAL shares rally as India's defence production hits all-time high

Key facts

  • India's defence production hits all-time high
  • Paras Defence, MTAR Tech, and HAL shares rallied
  • Milestone reflects Make in India and Aatmanirbhar Bharat push
  • Domestic production drive aims to cut import dependency

NDTV Profit reports that shares of Paras Defence, MTAR Technologies, and HAL surged in trade after the government announced that India's defence production has reached an all-time high. The announcement acted as a strong sentiment booster for the domestic defence manufacturing sector, which has been one of the key themes in Indian equity markets over recent years.

India has been aggressively pushing indigenisation of its defence supply chain, setting ambitious targets for domestic production and restricting imports of a range of military platforms and components. HAL, which manufactures fighter jets and helicopters, and companies like MTAR Tech and Paras Defence, which supply critical sub-systems, are direct beneficiaries of rising government orders.

For investors, defence stocks have been among the top performers on Indian exchanges over the past few years, underpinned by a multi-year government spending commitment. A confirmed all-time high in production reinforces confidence that order books for these companies will remain robust.
