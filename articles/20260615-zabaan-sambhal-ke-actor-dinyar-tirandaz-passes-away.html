Zabaan Sambhal Ke actor Dinyar Tirandaz passes away

What happened

The Indian Express reports that Dinyar Tirandaz, the actor widely recognised for his role in the classic Hindi sitcom Zabaan Sambhal Ke, has died. No further details about the cause or circumstances of his death were provided in the report.

The context

Zabaan Sambhal Ke was one of Doordarshan's most popular comedy series, running through the 1990s and earning a devoted national audience. Dinyar Tirandaz was a recognisable character actor whose work on the show made him a household name in an era before cable television fragmented viewership.

Why it matters

His death is a reminder of the thinning ranks of the actors who defined Indian public television comedy. For millions who grew up with Doordarshan, it is a genuinely felt cultural loss.

What's next

Expect tributes from the Indian television industry and fellow cast members of Zabaan Sambhal Ke in the coming days.
