Bharat Innovates 2026 Concludes in Nice, Showcasing India's Deep-Tech Ambitions on Global Stage

Key facts

  • Bharat Innovates 2026 held in Nice, France
  • Focused on positioning India as a global deep-tech and innovation hub
  • PIB coverage signals strong government backing for the initiative

PIB reports that Bharat Innovates 2026 has concluded in Nice, France, with the event serving as a major platform to strengthen India's image as a global hub for deep-tech innovation. The conclave featured Indian startups, researchers, and policymakers presenting India's technology capabilities to an international audience.

The event is part of a sustained government push to take India's innovation ecosystem to global forums, seeking foreign partnerships, investment, and visibility for Indian deep-tech ventures in sectors such as artificial intelligence, space technology, biotechnology, and advanced manufacturing.

India has been steadily building its deep-tech startup base, supported by government initiatives and growing private venture capital. Events like Bharat Innovates are designed to accelerate that momentum by connecting Indian innovators with global networks.

For everyday Indians, greater international recognition of India's innovation ecosystem can translate into increased foreign investment in homegrown companies, more high-value employment, and a stronger pipeline of technology-driven solutions to domestic challenges.
