Bharat Bhhagya Viddhaata witnesses 45% growth on Day 2 at the box office

What happened

The Indian Express reports that Bharat Bhhagya Viddhaata, starring Kangana Ranaut, witnessed a 45% growth in box office collections on Day 2 of its theatrical run. The film had opened to a modest first-day collection and the sharp uptick on the second day is being read as a positive audience signal.

The context

Kangana Ranaut is one of Bollywood's most high-profile and controversial actresses, whose recent films have had mixed box office results. A political drama release in the current climate draws considerable public attention, and opening-weekend momentum is widely seen as a key indicator of long-term theatrical viability.

Why it matters

A strong Day 2 growth for a Bollywood film can determine its theatrical run and affect downstream revenue from satellite and OTT rights, impacting both the production house and exhibitors. For Kangana Ranaut, it also carries personal brand significance given the scrutiny her releases attract.

What's next

Expect the film's first-week total to determine whether distributors and multiplex chains extend its screen count or begin winding down the run.
