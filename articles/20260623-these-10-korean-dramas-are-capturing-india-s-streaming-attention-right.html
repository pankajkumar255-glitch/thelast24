These 10 Korean dramas are capturing India's streaming attention right now

Key facts

  • India Today lists 10 Korean dramas trending on Indian streaming platforms
  • K-drama viewership has expanded beyond urban niche to mainstream Indian audiences
  • Korean content available on Netflix, Viki and other Indian OTT platforms
  • Cultural spillover includes K-pop, Korean food and language learning trends

Korean dramas have firmly cemented their place in the Indian streaming mainstream, and India Today's latest roundup of the ten K-dramas currently capturing the country's attention is a vivid snapshot of just how far this cultural wave has travelled. What began as a cult following among urban youth has expanded into a broad, cross-demographic audience that spans metros and smaller cities alike.

The popularity of Korean content on Indian OTT platforms has been building steadily for several years, accelerated by the global breakout of titles like Squid Game and the availability of subtitled K-dramas on Netflix, Viki, and other services. Indian viewers have responded to Korean drama's distinctive blend of melodrama, tight plotting, high production values, and emotional intensity — qualities that resonate with audiences already attuned to Indian soap operas but hungry for something visually slicker.

The trend has real cultural spillover effects: K-pop fandom, Korean beauty products, and Korean cuisine have all seen rising interest in India, often led by the same audiences who discovered the country through its dramas. Language-learning apps report Korean among the fastest-growing languages being studied by Indian users.

For the Indian entertainment industry, the K-drama phenomenon is both a competitive benchmark and a challenge — demonstrating that Indian audiences will invest deeply in foreign content if the storytelling is compelling enough, raising the bar for domestic OTT productions.
