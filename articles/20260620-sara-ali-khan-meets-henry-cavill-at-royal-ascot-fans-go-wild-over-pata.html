Sara Ali Khan meets Henry Cavill at Royal Ascot; fans go wild over 'Pataudi princess x Superman' moment

Key facts

  • Sara Ali Khan met Henry Cavill at Royal Ascot in England
  • Fans called it 'Pataudi princess x Superman' moment
  • Royal Ascot is a top-tier British celebrity and royalty event
  • The encounter went viral on Indian social media

The Indian Express reports that Sara Ali Khan met Hollywood actor Henry Cavill at Royal Ascot, the iconic British horse racing event attended by royalty and global celebrities. The encounter was captured and shared widely, with fans on social media gleefully dubbing it the 'Pataudi princess x Superman' moment — a nod to Sara's lineage as the granddaughter of cricketing legend Mansoor Ali Khan Pataudi, and to Cavill's most famous role as Superman in the DC films.

Royal Ascot, held annually at Ascot Racecourse in Berkshire, England, is one of the world's most prestigious sporting and social events, known for its strict dress code and high-profile attendees. Sara Ali Khan's presence there placed her among international celebrities at a marquee global gathering.

The viral moment underscores the growing visibility of Indian celebrities at global A-list events and the enthusiastic fan culture that instantly amplifies such cross-cultural celebrity encounters on platforms like Instagram and X.
