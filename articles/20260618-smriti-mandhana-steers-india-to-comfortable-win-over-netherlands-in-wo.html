Smriti Mandhana steers India to comfortable win over Netherlands in Women's T20 World Cup 2026

Key facts

  • India beat Netherlands in Women's T20 World Cup 2026
  • Smriti Mandhana was the standout performer
  • Result described as an easy win for India

Olympics.com reports that India's women's team registered a comfortable victory over the Netherlands in the Women's T20 World Cup 2026, with Smriti Mandhana leading the way with a commanding performance. The result underlines India's status as one of the tournament favourites, with the team displaying the kind of dominant batting-led cricket that has defined their recent T20 campaigns.

Mandhana, the left-handed opener and one of India's most consistent international batters, was the central figure in the win. Her ability to set up totals or chase down targets efficiently makes her form a critical indicator of how far this Indian side can go in the tournament.

The Netherlands, ranked significantly below India in the ICC women's rankings, were always expected to struggle, but the manner of the win — described as easy — will give Indian fans and the coaching staff confidence ahead of tougher knockout-stage encounters. India will now look to consolidate their position at the top of their group as the tournament progresses.
