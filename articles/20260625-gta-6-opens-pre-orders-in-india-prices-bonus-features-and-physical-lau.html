GTA 6 Opens Pre-Orders in India: Prices, Bonus Features and Physical Launch Details Revealed

Key facts

  • GTA 6 pre-orders now open in India
  • India-specific pricing and bonus features confirmed
  • Physical copies available at launch
  • Reported by NDTV Profit

Grand Theft Auto 6 has officially opened pre-orders in India, with platform-specific pricing, pre-order bonuses, and physical launch details now available for Indian buyers. The announcement, reported by NDTV Profit, marks a major milestone for India's gaming community, which has been among the most eager audiences globally for the sequel to Rockstar Games' blockbuster franchise.

Pre-order bonuses and physical edition specifics have been detailed for Indian consumers, giving buyers clarity on what to expect when the game launches. Physical copies will be available at launch alongside digital versions, catering to India's significant retail gaming segment.

GTA 6 is widely considered the most anticipated video game release in recent memory, with Rockstar Games having kept details tightly under wraps for years. The Indian gaming market has grown substantially in recent years, driven by affordable smartphones, expanding broadband access, and a younger demographic with rising disposable incomes.

The availability of pre-orders in India signals that global publishers are increasingly treating the Indian market as a first-tier launch territory rather than an afterthought. How the final pricing stacks up against global equivalents will be closely watched by Indian gaming communities and industry observers alike.
