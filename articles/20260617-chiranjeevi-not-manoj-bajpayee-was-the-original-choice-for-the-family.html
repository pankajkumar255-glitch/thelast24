Chiranjeevi, not Manoj Bajpayee, was the original choice for The Family Man, reveals director Raj Nidimoru

What happened

The Indian Express reports that director Raj Nidimoru revealed Chiranjeevi was the original choice to play the lead in The Family Man before Manoj Bajpayee was cast. Nidimoru made the disclosure publicly, highlighting how close the show came to having an entirely different lead actor. No further details about why Chiranjeevi did not take the role were specified in the headline.

The context

The Family Man, created by Raj Nidimoru and Krishna D.K. (Raj & DK) for Amazon Prime Video, premiered in 2019 and became a massive hit across India. Manoj Bajpayee's performance as Srikant Tiwari is widely regarded as one of the finest in Indian OTT history, earning him multiple awards. Chiranjeevi is one of Telugu cinema's biggest stars, and his casting would have given the show a very different tone and audience.

Why it matters

The revelation underscores how pivotal casting decisions shape iconic Indian pop culture properties — Bajpayee's everyman intensity was central to The Family Man's identity. Fans awaiting a third season will view this trivia through the lens of what the franchise has become.

What's next

Expect The Family Man Season 3, currently in production with Bajpayee, to draw renewed attention following this behind-the-scenes revelation.
