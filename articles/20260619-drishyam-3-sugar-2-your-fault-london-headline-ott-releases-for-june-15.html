Drishyam 3, Sugar 2, Your Fault: London headline OTT releases for June 15–21

Key facts

  • Drishyam 3 streams this week — major franchise release
  • Sugar 2 and Your Fault: London also dropping June 15–21
  • Content spread across Netflix, Prime Video, JioHotstar, ZEE5, Apple TV+

The Times of India and The Economic Times both report that the week of June 15 to 21 brings a packed OTT schedule across India's major streaming platforms. The standout release is Drishyam 3, the highly awaited sequel in the Mohanlal-led thriller franchise that has been one of Indian cinema's biggest franchises. Sugar 2 and Your Fault: London are also arriving this week, catering to audiences looking for drama and romance.

The titles are spread across Netflix, Prime Video, JioHotstar, ZEE5, and Apple TV+, giving subscribers of multiple platforms something to look forward to. The sheer variety — from a Malayalam franchise blockbuster to international productions — reflects the breadth of content now reaching Indian homes every week.

For everyday Indian viewers, this week represents exactly the kind of at-home entertainment boom that has made OTT subscriptions a household staple. Drishyam 3 in particular is expected to drive significant streaming numbers, given the cult following of its predecessors.
