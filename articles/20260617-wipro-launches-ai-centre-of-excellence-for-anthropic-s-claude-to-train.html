Wipro Launches AI Centre of Excellence for Anthropic's Claude, to Train 10,000 Experts

What happened

NDTV Profit reports that Wipro has launched an AI Centre of Excellence specifically built around Anthropic's Claude models and intends to train 10,000 of its employees as Claude experts. The centre is designed to help Wipro deliver Claude-based AI solutions to enterprise clients globally. The announcement comes amid broader industry moves by Indian IT firms to deepen alliances with leading AI model providers.

The context

Wipro is one of India's largest IT services companies, and the race among Indian IT majors to build specialised AI competencies has intensified as global enterprises accelerate AI adoption. Anthropic's Claude is a major competitor to OpenAI's GPT models and Google's Gemini in the enterprise AI space. Building a dedicated centre signals Wipro's intention to make Claude-powered services a core revenue stream.

Why it matters

The creation of 10,000 Claude-trained specialists at Wipro could create significant new employment and career pathways within India's IT sector, while also influencing which AI platforms dominate enterprise contracts globally. For Wipro clients, it signals faster and more specialised AI implementation support.

What's next

Expect other Indian IT majors such as Infosys and TCS to announce similar AI centres of excellence with competing model providers in the months ahead.
