India Women aim for perfect start against Pakistan in Women's T20 World Cup 2026

What happened

The Hindu reports that India Women are targeting a perfect start to their Women's T20 World Cup 2026 campaign against arch-rivals Pakistan. Olympics.com also previewed the match, detailing head-to-head records, match timings, and live streaming information for Indian viewers.

The context

India and Pakistan have a historically charged rivalry in women's T20 cricket, with India generally holding the edge in ICC tournaments. The Women's T20 World Cup has grown rapidly in stature, with the 2020 final in Australia drawing a record crowd. India's squad includes experienced players alongside emerging stars looking to make their mark.

Why it matters

A strong start against Pakistan would boost India Women's net run rate and confidence for the knockout rounds, while the match is expected to draw one of the highest viewership numbers of the group stage. It is also a showcase moment for the growth of women's cricket in India.

What's next

Expect the result of the India vs Pakistan Women's T20 World Cup clash to dominate sports headlines and social media conversation across India.
