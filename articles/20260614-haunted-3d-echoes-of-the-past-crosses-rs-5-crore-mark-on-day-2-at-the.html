Haunted 3D Echoes of the Past crosses Rs 5 crore mark on Day 2 at the box office

What happened

The Indian Express reports that Haunted 3D Echoes of the Past, featuring Mimoh Chakraborty, crossed the Rs 5 crore mark at the box office by Day 2 of its release. The horror film has been tracking positively in its opening weekend.

The context

The horror genre has seen a gradual revival in Indian cinema, with films like Stree and its sequels demonstrating that the genre can draw large multiplex audiences. Mimoh Chakraborty has been trying to build a consistent screen presence, and a commercially viable opening is important for his career momentum.

Why it matters

Crossing Rs 5 crore in two days gives the film a viable base to sustain its theatrical run through the week, which matters for both the production house's returns and for multiplex chains deciding screen allocation. It also signals continued audience interest in horror as a mainstream Bollywood genre.

What's next

Expect the film's first-week total to clarify whether it can sustain competition from other concurrent releases or will be overtaken quickly.
