Laapataa Ladies Actor Satendra Soni Alleges Death Threat on Set; Film Body Demands FIR

Key facts

  • Satendra Soni alleges a filmmaker threatened to kill him on set
  • Soni is known for his role in Laapataa Ladies
  • A film body has backed Soni and is seeking an FIR against the filmmaker

Satendra Soni, the actor who appeared in Kiran Rao's widely celebrated film Laapataa Ladies, has come forward with a serious allegation: that a filmmaker threatened to kill him while on set. The claim, which surfaced publicly, has triggered an immediate and visible response from within the film industry itself.

A film industry body has publicly backed Soni, voicing support for his account and formally demanding that authorities register a First Information Report against the filmmaker named in the allegation. The move is significant because industry bodies in Bollywood have historically been reluctant to take sides in disputes that pit established filmmakers against actors, especially those without leading-man status.

Soni gained wide recognition through Laapataa Ladies, Kiran Rao's return to direction which became one of the most talked-about Hindi films of its year and was India's entry for the Academy Awards. His visibility from that role appears to have given him a platform to raise the allegation publicly in a way that has drawn industry attention.

The demand for a formal FIR means the matter could now move into the criminal justice system, where the specific nature of the alleged threat would come under legal scrutiny. As of now, the named filmmaker has not publicly responded to the allegations. The story is developing, and whether police act on the film body's demand will be closely watched across the industry.
