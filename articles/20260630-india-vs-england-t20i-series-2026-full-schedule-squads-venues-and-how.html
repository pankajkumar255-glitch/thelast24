India vs England T20I series 2026: Full schedule, squads, venues and how to watch

Key facts

  • India vs England T20I series 2026 schedule and squads announced
  • Venues, IST timings and live streaming details confirmed
  • Madan Lal warns India the series 'won't be easy'

The full blueprint for the India vs England T20I series 2026 has been released, covering the schedule, venues, IST timings, squad lists from both nations, and live streaming options for Indian viewers. The series represents one of the most anticipated bilateral cricket engagements of the year, pitting two powerhouses of the shortest format against each other.

India will be eager to put behind a difficult run in recent ICC events, and a home series against England — one of the most technically equipped T20 sides in the world — will serve as both a test of character and a platform for new players to stake their claims. England, themselves a formidable white-ball unit, will arrive as serious challengers with no intention of making it comfortable for the hosts.

Former India fast bowler Madan Lal has already sounded a note of caution ahead of the series, warning that facing England will not be easy and urging the team not to take the opposition lightly. His message reflects a broader anxiety that India must not let their home advantage lull them into complacency.

With venues, timings, and streaming details now confirmed, fans across the country can plan their schedules around the series, which promises to be a high-octane contest and a crucial staging post for both squads as they build towards future ICC events.
