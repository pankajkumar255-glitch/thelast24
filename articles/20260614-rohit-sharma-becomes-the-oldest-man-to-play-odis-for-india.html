Rohit Sharma becomes the oldest man to play ODIs for India

What happened

India Today reports that Rohit Sharma created history by becoming the oldest man ever to play an ODI for India. The milestone was achieved during an ongoing ODI series. The record underscores Rohit's enduring place in Indian cricket's white-ball setup.

The context

Rohit Sharma has been one of India's most decorated white-ball cricketers, holding the record for the most ODI centuries. Questions about his longevity at the international level have been frequent, making this age-related record particularly notable. ODI cricket has seen many Indian veterans bow out in their early-to-mid thirties, making Rohit's continued selection at this stage significant.

Why it matters

The record signals that Rohit Sharma remains a selector-backed cornerstone of India's ODI plans, affecting team composition and the opportunities available to younger players. For fans, it is a milestone moment celebrating the career of one of India's most beloved cricketers.

What's next

Expect scrutiny of whether Rohit can sustain his form and fitness through the remainder of the series and beyond, with selectors likely watching his performances closely.
