Vaibhav Sooryavanshi falls for 21 again as consistency concerns mount against Sri Lanka A

What happened

The Times of India reports that Vaibhav Sooryavanshi was dismissed for 21 against Sri Lanka A during the Tri-Nation Series 2026, describing it as 'another start, another miss' — indicating a recurring pattern of the youngster getting in and then getting out cheaply.

The context

Vaibhav Sooryavanshi burst onto the scene as one of Indian cricket's most exciting young prospects, attracting widespread attention for his aggressive batting style. The India A versus Sri Lanka A series is a key proving ground for players on the fringes of senior national selection.

Why it matters

Repeated failure to convert starts at the A level could delay Sooryavanshi's path to the full India squad, dampening the excitement around one of domestic cricket's most watched youngsters. Fans and selectors will be watching closely to see if this is a blip or a deeper technical issue.

What's next

Expect scrutiny to intensify around Sooryavanshi's technique and temperament as the Tri-Nation A series progresses.
