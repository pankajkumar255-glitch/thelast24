Drishyam 3, Athiradi and more: South Indian OTT releases to watch this week

What happened

Moneycontrol reports that this week's South Indian OTT releases include Drishyam 3 and Athiradi, alongside other titles. The roundup highlights the growing volume and variety of regional language content now available to Indian streaming audiences.

The context

The rise of South Indian cinema on OTT platforms accelerated sharply after pan-India hits like RRR, KGF and Baahubali demonstrated massive national and global demand for Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam and Kannada content. Streaming platforms now invest heavily in South Indian originals and acquisitions to serve this audience.

Why it matters

The steady stream of South Indian OTT releases expands viewing choices for Hindi-belt audiences while also ensuring regional language speakers across India get timely access to their preferred content without depending on theatrical releases.

What's next

Expect competition among OTT platforms to intensify as they vie for exclusive rights to upcoming South Indian blockbusters in the second half of 2025.
