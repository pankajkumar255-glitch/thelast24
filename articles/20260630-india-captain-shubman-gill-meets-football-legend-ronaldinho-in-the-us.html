India Captain Shubman Gill Meets Football Legend Ronaldinho in the US

Key facts

  • India captain Shubman Gill met Ronaldinho in the United States
  • Gill is on tour with the Indian cricket team in the US
  • Ronaldinho is a Brazilian football icon

India captain Shubman Gill made headlines off the pitch during India's US tour after meeting Brazilian football legend Ronaldinho, with NDTV Sports reporting the encounter that quickly went viral on social media.

Gill, who has taken over leadership of the Indian cricket team, was in the United States as part of the team's touring commitments when he crossed paths with the iconic former Barcelona and Brazil forward. Ronaldinho, widely regarded as one of the most gifted footballers of his generation, is a global sporting superstar whose appeal transcends sport and geography.

The meeting drew considerable attention online, with fans celebrating the collision of two sporting worlds. For Gill, it was a rare moment away from the intensity of international cricket — though the image of India's cricket captain alongside a football legend also spoke to how much Indian cricket's global footprint has grown.

Gill has been at the centre of India's cricketing ambitions as captain, and his off-field profile continues to rise. Moments like these serve as a reminder that India's current cricket stars are increasingly recognised figures on the world stage, mingling with icons from other sports during their international tours.
