Rajinikanth's Enthiran to Gaganachari: 7 South Indian Sci-Fi Films Worth Watching on OTT

What happened

Pinkvilla has published a list of seven South Indian sci-fi films recommended for OTT viewing, including Rajinikanth's Enthiran and the Malayalam film Gaganachari, spanning multiple South Indian languages and eras of filmmaking.

The context

Enthiran, directed by S. Shankar and starring Rajinikanth alongside Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, was a landmark moment for Indian cinema's engagement with big-budget science fiction and remains one of the highest-grossing Tamil films ever made. South Indian cinema's sci-fi output has grown steadily since, with Malayalam and Telugu films increasingly joining Tamil productions in the genre.

Why it matters

Curated OTT lists like this drive significant new viewership to older and lesser-known regional films, helping sustain their commercial life and introducing them to audiences who may have missed their theatrical runs. It also reinforces the growing cross-language appeal of South Indian genre cinema.

What's next

Expect streaming numbers for titles on such curated lists to see a short-term spike as readers act on the recommendations over the coming weekend.
