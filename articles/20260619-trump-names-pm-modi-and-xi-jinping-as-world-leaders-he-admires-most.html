Trump names PM Modi and Xi Jinping as world leaders he admires most

Key facts

  • Trump named PM Modi and Xi Jinping as most admired world leaders
  • Statement comes amid ongoing US-China trade and geopolitical tensions
  • Trump and Modi have previously held multiple high-profile bilateral meetings

NDTV reports that US President Donald Trump, responding to a question about which world leader he admires most, cited Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Chinese President Xi Jinping. The statement is striking given that US-China relations remain deeply strained over trade, technology, and geopolitical competition, while US-India ties are generally on a positive trajectory.

Trump and Modi have cultivated a visible personal rapport across multiple meetings, including high-profile events in both countries. Trump's praise for Xi, however, comes amid ongoing tariff battles and tensions in the Indo-Pacific, making the pairing of the two names diplomatically unusual.

For India, a public endorsement of this nature from a sitting US president carries symbolic weight, reinforcing the warmth in bilateral ties. It also underscores Modi's stature on the global stage, even as India navigates its relationships carefully between Washington and Beijing.
