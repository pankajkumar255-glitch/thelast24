OTT releases this week: Drishyam 3, Sugar 2 and Your Fault: London arrive June 15–21

Key facts

  • Drishyam 3 streams this week, June 15–21
  • Sugar 2 and Your Fault: London also release this week
  • Drishyam 2 (2022) crossed Rs 200 crore at box office

The week of June 15 to 21 brings one of the most anticipated OTT drops of the year: Drishyam 3, the next chapter in Ajay Devgn's hugely popular crime-thriller franchise, arrives on streaming platforms alongside Sugar 2 and the international production Your Fault: London, according to Times of India's weekly OTT roundup.

Drishyam 3 is comfortably the headline attraction. The first film, a remake of the Malayalam original, was a massive hit, and Drishyam 2 — released theatrically in 2022 — crossed Rs 200 crore at the box office, cementing Vijay Salgaonkar as one of Hindi cinema's most beloved protagonists. The third instalment is expected to continue unravelling the consequences of the Salgaonkar family's desperate cover-up, and its streaming debut will draw enormous viewership.

Your Fault: London, the English-language sequel to the Spanish-language hit Your Fault, extends the Wattpad-adapted romance franchise into new territory, tapping into the same young-adult audience that drove the original's success on Netflix. Sugar 2 rounds out a week that offers genuine variety — from Indian crime drama to international romance.

For Indian streaming audiences, weeks anchored by a title as recognisable as Drishyam 3 tend to drive significant subscription activity and platform traffic. The staggered rollout of big franchise titles to OTT remains a key strategy for platforms competing fiercely for subscriber attention in the mid-year window.
