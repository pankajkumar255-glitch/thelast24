Taare Zameen Par child star Darsheel Safary's co-star has become a gold-medal dentist and pilot in the UK

What happened

The Indian Express reports that the actor who played Yohaan in the 2007 Bollywood film Taare Zameen Par has left the film industry and is now based in the UK, where he has won a gold medal in dentistry and also qualified as a pilot.

The context

Taare Zameen Par, directed by and starring Aamir Khan, was a landmark Hindi film about a dyslexic child and became one of Bollywood's most emotionally resonant hits. The film launched the career of child actor Darsheel Safary and featured several young co-stars whose subsequent lives have rarely been in the public eye.

Why it matters

The story highlights the difficult transition child actors face after early fame, and offers a rare positive example of a former child star thriving in a completely different field. It resonates with Indian parents and fans who remember the film fondly.

What's next

Expect the story to go viral on social media as Taare Zameen Par fans rediscover where the film's young cast members are today.
