Drishyam 3, Your Fault: London and more: The biggest new OTT releases this week (June 15–21)

What happened

The Times of India reports that the week of June 15–21 brings a strong crop of new OTT releases, with Drishyam 3 leading the charge alongside Your Fault: London and Thukra Ke Mera Pyaar 2, among other titles across platforms.

The context

The Drishyam franchise, starring Ajay Devgn and directed by Abhishek Pathak, is one of Hindi cinema's most bankable thriller series, with the first two instalments posting massive box-office numbers. Its OTT release is typically one of the most-watched events on the streaming calendar for Hindi film fans.

Why it matters

A high-profile OTT week anchored by a franchise as popular as Drishyam drives significant subscription and viewership activity, benefiting whichever platform has secured its streaming rights. It also means millions of Indian households have major premium content to look forward to this weekend.

What's next

Expect Drishyam 3's OTT viewership numbers to be closely watched by the industry as a benchmark for how well theatrical blockbusters translate to streaming audiences.
