Sunil Gavaskar singles out an India player — not Shubman Gill — as a 'huge plus' for any team

What happened

NDTV Sports reports that Sunil Gavaskar has praised a specific India cricketer — emphasising it is not Shubman Gill — calling them a 'huge plus for any team'. The report does not name the player in the headline, but Gavaskar's endorsement has drawn considerable attention in cricket circles.

The context

Sunil Gavaskar is India's first cricketer to score 10,000 Test runs and remains one of the most respected commentators and analysts of the game. Shubman Gill recently took over India's Test captaincy, making him the natural focal point of team discussions. Gavaskar's decision to highlight another player signals that India's squad depth and emerging talent remain talking points ahead of upcoming international commitments.

Why it matters

Gavaskar's public praise can influence how selectors, sponsors, and fans view a player's value, potentially shaping team composition decisions. For followers of Indian cricket, it is a prompt to look beyond the marquee names at who is quietly making themselves indispensable.

What's next

Expect the identity and performance of the praised player to be closely scrutinised when India's next squad is announced.
