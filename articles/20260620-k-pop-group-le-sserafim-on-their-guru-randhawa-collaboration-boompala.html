K-pop group LE SSERAFIM on their Guru Randhawa collaboration 'Boompala', new single 'Supergirl', and upcoming India concert

Key facts

  • LE SSERAFIM collaborated with Guru Randhawa on track 'Boompala'
  • Group also announced new song 'Supergirl'
  • India concert plans revealed in exclusive Hindustan Times interview
  • LE SSERAFIM is signed to HYBE's SOURCE MUSIC label

Hindustan Times reports an exclusive interview with South Korean K-pop group LE SSERAFIM, in which the group opens up about their collaboration with Indian pop star Guru Randhawa on the track 'Boompala'. The interview also covers their new song 'Supergirl' and the group's plans for a live concert in India, underlining their growing engagement with the Indian market.

LE SSERAFIM, signed to SOURCE MUSIC under the HYBE label — the same label behind BTS — are among the top-tier fourth-generation K-pop groups globally. Guru Randhawa is one of India's most streamed pop and Punjabi music artists, making the 'Boompala' collaboration a significant cross-cultural musical moment.

The announcement of an India concert will be particularly exciting for the group's Indian fanbase, often referred to as 'FEARNOTs'. As K-pop's influence in India continues to surge — reflected in chart numbers, streaming data, and sold-out merchandise — this interview highlights how international acts are now treating India as a priority concert destination rather than an afterthought.
