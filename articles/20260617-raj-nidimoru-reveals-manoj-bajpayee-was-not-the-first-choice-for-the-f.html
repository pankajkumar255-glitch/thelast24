Raj Nidimoru reveals Manoj Bajpayee was not the first choice for The Family Man

What happened

NDTV reports that Raj Nidimoru, one half of the directing duo Raj & DK behind The Family Man, revealed in an interview that another actor was approached and considered before Manoj Bajpayee was cast as the lead. Nidimoru did not publicly name the original first choice in the headline, but confirmed Bajpayee was not the initial selection.

The context

The Family Man, created by Raj & DK and streaming on Amazon Prime Video, debuted in 2019 and became a massive critical and commercial hit, with Bajpayee's performance universally praised. The show ran for two seasons and is widely regarded as a benchmark for Indian OTT content. Casting decisions of this magnitude often reshape an actor's career trajectory significantly.

Why it matters

The disclosure underscores how pivotal casting choices are to a show's identity, and adds to the cultural conversation around The Family Man as fans and industry watchers speculate about who the original choice was.

What's next

Expect the internet to speculate heavily on the unnamed first-choice actor, and likely further revelations as Raj & DK continue promotional activities.
