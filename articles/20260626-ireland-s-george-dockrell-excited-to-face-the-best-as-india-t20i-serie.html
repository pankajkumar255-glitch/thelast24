Ireland's George Dockrell excited to face 'the best' as India T20I series begins

Key facts

  • George Dockrell says he is excited to test himself against 'the best'
  • Dockrell is one of Ireland's most experienced international cricketers
  • Comments made ahead of Ireland vs India T20I series 2026

George Dockrell, Ireland's seasoned all-rounder, has set the tone for his side's T20I series against India with a declaration of intent, telling The Hindu he is genuinely excited to pit himself against 'the best' as the two countries prepare to face off.

Dockrell's comments reflect both personal ambition and a broader confidence within Irish cricket that has developed steadily over years of competing at the highest level. Having featured in landmark Irish victories in international cricket over the past decade, he brings experience and a competitive edge to what could be a challenging series against one of the game's powerhouses.

For Ireland, the series is an opportunity to demonstrate their T20 credentials on a bigger stage. They have consistently punched above their weight in global cricket, and while India — bolstered by talents like Vaibhav Sooryavanshi — will be strong favourites, the Irish will be determined not to simply make up the numbers.

Dockrell's eagerness to take on top-quality opposition speaks to the culture of ambition that has helped Ireland earn Full Member status with the ICC and qualify for major tournaments. His mindset, and that of the broader Irish squad, will be central to whether they can spring a surprise when the series gets underway.
