Deepti Sharma steers India Women to commanding win over Pakistan in T20 World Cup 2026

What happened

Olympics.com reports that India Women defeated Pakistan Women in their T20 World Cup 2026 group-stage fixture, with Deepti Sharma playing a lead role in guiding India to a comfortable win. The report describes the victory as an easy one for the Indian side.

The context

The Women's T20 World Cup is one of ICC's flagship events and the India-Pakistan fixture is invariably its most-watched match. Deepti Sharma is a senior India Women all-rounder known for her off-spin bowling and useful lower-order batting. India Women have been among the consistent performers in women's T20 cricket in recent years.

Why it matters

A win over Pakistan boosts India's net run rate and positioning in the group stage, improving their chances of advancing to the knockout rounds. For the millions of women's cricket fans in India, this result is a significant morale booster for the national team.

What's next

Expect India Women to build on this momentum as they face tougher opponents in the later stages of the group phase of the T20 World Cup 2026.
