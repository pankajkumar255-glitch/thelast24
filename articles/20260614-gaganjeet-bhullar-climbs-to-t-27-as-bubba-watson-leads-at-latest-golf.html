Gaganjeet Bhullar climbs to T-27 as Bubba Watson leads at latest golf tournament

What happened

Sportstar reports that Gaganjeet Bhullar climbed to the tied-27th position at the tournament covered in the Indian sports wrap for June 14. Bubba Watson currently holds a one-shot lead at the top of the leaderboard. The update is part of Sportstar's daily summary of Indian sporting action.

The context

Gaganjeet Bhullar is one of India's most experienced and decorated professional golfers on the international circuit, with multiple Asian Tour victories to his name. Bubba Watson is a two-time Masters champion and a well-known name in global golf. International golf tournaments featuring Indian participants attract a niche but growing audience in India.

Why it matters

Bhullar's steady climb keeps alive the prospect of a strong finish, which would be a boost for the visibility of Indian golf on the world stage. A high finish would also enhance Bhullar's world ranking and eligibility for bigger tournaments.

What's next

Expect the final rounds to determine whether Bhullar can make a push up the leaderboard and whether Watson can hold off challengers to claim the title.
