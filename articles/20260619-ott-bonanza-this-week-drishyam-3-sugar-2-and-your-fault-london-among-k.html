OTT Bonanza This Week: Drishyam 3, Sugar 2, and Your Fault: London Among Key Releases

Key facts

  • Drishyam 3 is the headline OTT release of June 15–21
  • Sugar 2 (season 2) also drops the same week
  • Your Fault: London is the international addition to the week's slate
  • Regional titles including Malayalam film Kenatha Kanom also release this week

The Times of India reports that the week of June 15 to 21 brings one of the more stacked OTT release calendars of the year, with Drishyam 3 leading the charge as the most anticipated title. The film is the third instalment in the blockbuster Drishyam franchise, which has built a massive following across India for its tightly plotted crime-thriller narrative.

Alongside it, Sugar 2 arrives as the second season of the thriller series, while Your Fault: London — the London-set continuation of the Spanish-origin romantic drama — adds an international flavour to the week's offerings. The Economic Times separately highlights regional language content arriving the same week, including the Malayalam title Kenatha Kanom, underlining the breadth of South Indian OTT releases running parallel.

For everyday Indian viewers, the sheer variety — franchise sequels, international co-productions, and regional originals — across a single week reflects how crowded and competitive the Indian streaming market has become. With multiple platforms vying for attention, this week offers genuine choice across languages, genres, and moods.
