India vs New Zealand: Squads announced for ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2026 Final

What happened

Cricbuzz reports that squads for the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2026 Final between India and New Zealand have been officially announced. The publication details the full list of players named by both sides ahead of the title decider. India's inclusion in the final marks another deep run in an ICC knockout event. New Zealand, perennial contenders in global tournaments, will line up as formidable opponents in what is the sport's showpiece T20 occasion.

The context

The ICC Men's T20 World Cup is the premier international T20 tournament, held biennially and featuring all major cricketing nations. India and New Zealand have a storied rivalry in ICC events, with New Zealand having knocked India out of multiple World Cups including the 2019 ODI World Cup semi-final and the inaugural World Test Championship Final in 2021. India won the T20 World Cup in 2007 and most recently in 2024, while New Zealand have been runners-up in multiple ICC tournaments without claiming the T20 title. Finals between these two sides carry enormous viewership and commercial weight.

Why it matters

A T20 World Cup final featuring India commands hundreds of millions of viewers domestically and massive advertising revenue. For fans, it is the culmination of a tournament — the result will define India's ICC trophy count and shape the narrative around the current squad. A win would be India's back-to-back T20 World Cup title, cementing this generation's legacy.

What's next

Expect intense debate around India's final playing XI selection, with key decisions on batting order and bowling combinations likely to dominate headlines in the build-up. The match itself will be watched closely for conditions, toss decisions, and whether star players deliver under the ultimate pressure.
