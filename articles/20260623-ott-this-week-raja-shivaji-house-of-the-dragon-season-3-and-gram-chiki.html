OTT This Week: 'Raja Shivaji', 'House of the Dragon' Season 3 and 'Gram Chikitsalay' Season 2 Among New Releases

Key facts

  • House of the Dragon Season 3 drops this week
  • Raja Shivaji (historical drama) is a new Indian OTT release
  • Gram Chikitsalay Season 2 returns
  • South Indian titles include Baahubali: The Torchbearer, Blast, and Lingam
  • Releases span June 22–28, 2026

The week of June 22 to 28, 2026 shapes up as one of the busier OTT weeks of the month, with platforms releasing a diverse mix of Indian and international content simultaneously. Leading the charge is House of the Dragon Season 3, the HBO fantasy series that continues the Game of Thrones universe and remains one of the most globally anticipated shows on streaming.

On the Indian originals front, Raja Shivaji brings a historical drama lens to the legacy of the Maratha king, catering to an audience that has shown strong appetite for period storytelling — a genre that has thrived on OTT since Tandav and The Empire proved its viability. Gram Chikitsalay returns for a second season, its rural healthcare setting having carved a niche following in its debut run.

South Indian OTT content also figures prominently this week, with titles including Baahubali: The Torchbearer and Arjun Sarja's Blast and Lingam among the new additions, underlining how South Indian content continues to dominate streaming charts and drive subscriber numbers for regional-language platforms.

For viewers navigating multiple subscriptions, the week offers genuine reasons to explore across platforms. With so many titles competing for attention between June 22 and 28, the shows that generate the strongest word-of-mouth in the first 48 hours will likely define which ones break into broader cultural conversation.
