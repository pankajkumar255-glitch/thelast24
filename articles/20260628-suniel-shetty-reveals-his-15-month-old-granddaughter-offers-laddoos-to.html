Suniel Shetty reveals his 15-month-old granddaughter offers laddoos to PM Modi's photo every day

Key facts

  • Suniel Shetty's granddaughter is 15 months old
  • She reportedly offers laddoos to PM Modi's photo daily
  • Shetty shared the anecdote amid Welcome to the Jungle's release

Suniel Shetty, one of Bollywood's most enduring stars and currently appearing in the ensemble comedy Welcome to the Jungle, has shared a delightful anecdote that has quickly warmed social media: his 15-month-old granddaughter offers laddoos to a photograph of Prime Minister Narendra Modi every single day.

The actor, speaking publicly about his family life, described the daily ritual with evident affection. The image of a toddler making such an offering — apparently as a matter of habitual routine — has resonated widely, blending the worlds of celebrity, family, and patriotic sentiment in a manner that feels entirely unforced.

Shetty has been a prominent presence in entertainment news this week, given the release of Welcome to the Jungle, in which he stars alongside Akshay Kumar. The film's strong opening day performance has put both actors back in the commercial spotlight, and Shetty's personal charm — evident in this kind of candid family disclosure — continues to endear him to audiences well beyond his action-hero heyday.

The anecdote, while light, speaks to the actor's grounded family life and the affection he clearly holds for his grandchild. It is the kind of story that travels far beyond film circles and connects with everyday Indian audiences who find in it something recognisably warm and human.
