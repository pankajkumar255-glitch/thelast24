Pune Fort Murder: Siya Goyal and Chetan Chaudhary Googled Murder Methods, Rehearsed Pushing Ketan — Police

Key facts

  • Siya Goyal and Chetan Chaudhary searched murder methods on Google
  • Duo allegedly rehearsed how to push victim Ketan Agarwal
  • Viral cricket stadium video places accused together
  • Ujjwal Nikam appointed Special Public Prosecutor in case
  • Siya allegedly said killing was easier than telling family

Pune Police have revealed chilling details in the Ketan Agarwal murder case, disclosing that accused Siya Goyal and Chetan Chaudhary searched for murder methods on Google and physically rehearsed how to push Ketan before executing the plan at a fort. Investigators say the two had been in a secret relationship, and that Siya allegedly confessed it felt easier to kill her fiancé than to disclose the affair to her family.

A viral video from a cricket stadium has emerged as crucial evidence, placing Siya and Chetan together and contradicting any claims of innocence. The cricket connection — described in multiple reports as the thread that first brought the two together — now forms a central plank of the police investigation.

The FIR details allege a calculated, premeditated killing rather than a crime of passion, with the duo's Google search history and physical rehearsals pointing to advance planning. The brother of the accused also reportedly made significant disclosures to police, adding another dimension to the investigation.

The case has gripped national attention for its disturbing details — a young woman accused of conspiring to murder her own fiancé. Renowned prosecutor Ujjwal Nikam, known for the 26/11 trial, has been appointed as special public prosecutor in the case, signalling the gravity with which authorities are treating it.
