Irfan Pathan urges Rishabh Pant to draw inspiration from a fellow India star

What happened

NDTV Sports reports that former India cricketer Irfan Pathan has publicly advised Rishabh Pant to take inspiration from a specific current India star, though the headline does not name that player. The advice appears aimed at helping Pant sharpen his consistency at the international level as he continues his comeback across formats.

The context

Rishabh Pant suffered life-threatening injuries in a road accident in December 2022 and made a remarkable return to international cricket in 2024. He has since played Test, ODI and T20I cricket for India but has faced scrutiny over shot selection and consistency. Irfan Pathan is a respected cricket analyst and former India player whose opinions carry public weight.

Why it matters

Pant is considered central to India's batting plans across formats, and any public debate about his approach or mindset shapes how selectors and fans evaluate his place in the squad. His form directly influences India's chances in high-stakes tournaments.

What's next

Expect Pant's performances in the ongoing bilateral series against Afghanistan and Ireland to be closely watched by selectors and commentators alike.
