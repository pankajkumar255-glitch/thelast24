Afghanistan captain Hashmatullah Shahidi calls India very tough team in home conditions ahead of ODI series

What happened

Rediff reports that Afghanistan captain Hashmatullah Shahidi has acknowledged the difficulty of taking on India in home conditions, describing India as a very tough team on their own turf ahead of the ongoing ODI series.

The context

Afghanistan have grown rapidly as a cricketing force, producing world-class spin bowlers and batters over the last decade. Playing in India, however, remains a different challenge given the conditions, crowds, and the quality of the Indian lineup. The bilateral series is part of both teams' preparation for upcoming ICC events.

Why it matters

The series gives India a chance to assess fringe players and test combinations, while for Afghanistan, performing well against India at home would be a major confidence boost for their ICC ambitions.

What's next

Expect Afghanistan to target India's middle order with their spin attack, while India will look to give more players game time across the remaining ODIs.
