PM Modi Arrives in France for Bilateral Talks and G7 Summit Engagements

What happened

Business Standard reports that PM Modi has reached France for bilateral talks alongside his engagements at the G7 Summit. The visit involves direct diplomatic meetings with French leadership as well as multilateral interactions with G7 heads of state.

The context

India is not a formal G7 member but has been invited as an outreach partner nation at multiple summits. France is one of India's key strategic partners, with deep ties in defence, space, and nuclear energy. The G7 this year is hosted by Canada.

Why it matters

Outcomes from these meetings can influence India's trade negotiations, defence procurement, and stance on global issues such as the Russia-Ukraine conflict and climate finance. Bilateral agreements with France often translate into technology transfers and investment flows into India.

What's next

Expect announcements on bilateral cooperation deals and watch for Modi's interventions at G7 sessions on climate, debt relief, and the global order.
