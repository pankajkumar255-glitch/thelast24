Lorcan Tucker named Ireland captain for T20I series against India

Key facts

  • Lorcan Tucker named Ireland T20I captain vs India
  • Tucker is Ireland's wicketkeeper-batter and an experienced white-ball player
  • Series is Ireland's most high-profile T20 bilateral assignment

Lorcan Tucker has been confirmed as Ireland's captain for the T20 International series against India, The Hindu reported. The wicketkeeper-batter, one of Ireland's most experienced white-ball cricketers, will lead the side as they prepare to take on one of the most demanding opponents in world T20 cricket.

Tucker's appointment reflects Ireland's belief in him not just as a performer behind the stumps and with the bat, but as a leader capable of marshalling a young squad against elite opposition. His calm demeanour and familiarity with pressure situations across Ireland's recent international fixtures make him a natural choice for the captaincy.

The T20I series against India is one of the most high-profile assignments in Ireland's cricket calendar, offering them prime exposure and the chance to upset a side that, while rotating its squad at times, never lacks for depth or quality. For India, the series is a platform to try combinations and blood newer players — a context in which Ireland will be looking to take full advantage of any opportunity given.

Details of the series schedule — match dates and venues — were separately confirmed by The Indian Express, with India vs Ireland T20 Series 2026 fixtures available for fans tracking the upcoming international action.
