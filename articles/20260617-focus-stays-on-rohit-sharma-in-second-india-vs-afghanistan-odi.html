Focus stays on Rohit Sharma in second India vs Afghanistan ODI

What happened

Hindustan Times reports that focus remains firmly on Rohit Sharma ahead of the second India vs Afghanistan ODI. The report suggests that Rohit's performance continues to be the central talking point of this series, with the team and selectors watching closely. Afghanistan captain Hashmatullah Shahidi had also acknowledged India as a very tough team in their own conditions, as reported by Rediff.

The context

Rohit Sharma is India's ODI captain and one of the most prolific batsmen in the format's history. His form in recent months has been under scrutiny as India look to build a strong squad for future ICC events. Afghanistan, under Hashmatullah Shahidi, have grown into a competitive ODI side capable of upsetting established teams.

Why it matters

Rohit's consistency in this series will be a key data point for selectors finalising India's ODI squad for upcoming tournaments, directly affecting team composition millions of fans care about. A strong performance would also settle public debate about his place and fitness.

What's next

Expect the second ODI to draw massive viewership as fans and selectors alike watch to see whether Rohit converts this opportunity into a significant score.
