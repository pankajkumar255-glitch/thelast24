Brett Lee Calls Mohanlal Co-Star Madhavan 'A Genuinely Top Bloke' After Dubai Golf Outing

Key facts

  • Brett Lee called Madhavan 'a genuinely top bloke'
  • The two met for a golf outing in Dubai
  • Lee shared pictures from the meeting on social media

The Indian Express reports that former Australian cricket fast bowler Brett Lee has publicly praised actor R. Madhavan after the two met for a golf outing in Dubai. Lee shared photographs from the meet-up and described Madhavan as 'a genuinely top bloke', a warm endorsement that quickly attracted attention on social media.

Madhavan is one of Indian cinema's most recognisable faces, known for his work in Tamil blockbusters as well as Hindi films including the 3 Idiots franchise, and more recently for Rocketry: The Nambi Effect. Brett Lee, meanwhile, has long maintained a close association with India — having played in the IPL and being widely loved by Indian cricket fans — making this pairing particularly resonant for Indian audiences.

The friendly exchange is a lighthearted celebrity moment that crosses the worlds of international cricket and Indian cinema, two of the country's biggest cultural passions.
