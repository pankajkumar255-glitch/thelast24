Kieron Pollard breaks Chris Gayle's all-time T20 run record, dedicates milestone to 'Universe Boss'

Key facts

  • Pollard breaks Chris Gayle's all-time T20 run-scoring record
  • Pollard's tribute: 'Sorry, Universe Boss'
  • Both players are West Indian T20 legends with deep IPL history

Kieron Pollard has etched his name into T20 cricketing history by surpassing Chris Gayle's record for the most runs in the format, becoming the all-time leading run-scorer in T20 cricket. In a moment full of sportsmanship and warmth, the Trinidad-born all-rounder sent a direct message to Gayle: 'Sorry, Universe Boss.'

Gayle's record had long seemed unassailable, set across hundreds of T20 appearances in leagues around the world including the IPL, where both men built legendary reputations. Pollard's longevity and consistency across franchise cricket — including his storied career with Mumbai Indians in the IPL — made him the natural challenger to the record, but its eventual breaking is no less extraordinary.

The Times of India reported Pollard's tribute to Gayle, which was as gracious as it was celebratory. The two West Indian legends have been contemporaries and friends, and Pollard's acknowledgement of Gayle's iconic status even as he surpassed him underscored the respect that defines their relationship.

For Indian cricket fans, who watched Pollard terrorise bowling attacks in the IPL for over a decade, this milestone carries particular resonance. It is a reminder that T20 cricket, a format India helped popularise, continues to produce history-making moments that transcend any single league or country.
