Drishyam 3 crosses Rs 330 crore worldwide, OTT release date announced

What happened

The Indian Express reports that Drishyam 3 has crossed Rs 330 crore in worldwide box office collections and that an OTT release date has been officially announced. The film stars Ajay Devgn and continues the long-running mystery-thriller franchise. No specific OTT platform or streaming date was named in the headline.

The context

Drishyam 3 is the latest entry in the franchise that began with the 2015 Hindi remake of the Malayalam original, starring Ajay Devgn as Vijay Salgaonkar. The series has built a massive fanbase on the strength of its suspense writing and family drama, with each instalment outperforming the last at the box office.

Why it matters

A Rs 330 crore-plus worldwide haul signals robust audience appetite for original Hindi thriller franchises, encouraging studios to greenlight more such projects. The OTT announcement now brings the film to the vast majority of Indian viewers who prefer streaming over theatre visits.

What's next

Expect the OTT premiere to drive a fresh wave of audience discussion and potentially push the franchise's total digital viewership to record numbers.
