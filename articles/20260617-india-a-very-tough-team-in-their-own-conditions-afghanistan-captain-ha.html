'India a Very Tough Team in Their Own Conditions': Afghanistan Captain Hashmatullah Shahidi

What happened

Rediff.com reports that Afghanistan captain Hashmatullah Shahidi described India as a very tough team in their own conditions. The remarks reflect Shahidi's assessment ahead of what appears to be a forthcoming contest involving both nations. No specific match or series was detailed in the headline.

The context

India has historically been one of the most difficult teams to beat on home soil across all formats of cricket, with their spin-friendly pitches and large crowds providing a significant advantage. Afghanistan has grown rapidly as a cricketing nation and has pulled off notable upsets in international cricket in recent years. Hashmatullah Shahidi is a respected Test and ODI batter who has led Afghanistan through a period of significant growth.

Why it matters

Afghanistan's growing competitiveness means their captain's words carry genuine weight — any upset result against India would be a major sporting event and would further elevate Afghanistan's cricket standing globally.
