Top 7 classic Indian TV shows available on OTT

Key facts

  • India Today lists 7 classic Indian TV shows now on OTT platforms
  • Includes shows originally aired on Doordarshan and early cable TV
  • OTT platforms using legacy content to expand subscriber bases
  • Classic shows accessible to younger audiences for the first time digitally

Decades of classic Indian television are now just a tap away, and India Today has assembled a guide to seven of the most iconic shows from Indian TV history that viewers can stream right now on OTT platforms. The list covers some of the most culturally resonant series ever made for Indian audiences — programmes that defined appointment viewing for entire generations.

The migration of classic Indian TV content to streaming platforms has been quietly accelerating over the past few years, as OTT services recognise that legacy content carries both nostalgia value for older subscribers and discovery value for younger ones who missed the original broadcasts. Titles that once aired on Doordarshan or early cable channels are being digitised and licensed for streaming.

The move also serves a cultural preservation function. Many of these shows represent high points of Indian television craft — writing, direction, and performance that holds up across time — and their availability on OTT ensures they are not lost to the analogue era.

For streaming platforms competing intensely for Indian subscriber attention, curating and surfacing classic content alongside new originals is an increasingly important strategy — one that costs far less to acquire than fresh productions but delivers significant viewer engagement.
