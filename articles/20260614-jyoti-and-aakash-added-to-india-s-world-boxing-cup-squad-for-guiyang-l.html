Jyoti and Aakash added to India's World Boxing Cup squad for Guiyang leg

What happened

Sportstar reports that boxers Jyoti and Aakash have been named in India's squad for the World Boxing Cup taking place in Guiyang, China. The announcement was part of the broader Indian sports wrap for June 13, 2026, confirming the two boxers' inclusion for the upcoming leg of the competition.

The context

The World Boxing Cup is an international boxing competition that provides competitive exposure for boxers outside the Olympics cycle. India has a strong boxing tradition, particularly after multiple Olympic and Commonwealth Games medals. Guiyang, China hosts this leg, offering Indian boxers valuable experience against international competition.

Why it matters

International exposure at events like the World Boxing Cup is critical for Indian boxers building their rankings and form ahead of the 2028 Los Angeles Olympics qualification cycle.

What's next

Expect Jyoti and Aakash to use the Guiyang leg as a key opportunity to impress selectors and build momentum toward future international championships.
