'Dhurandhar 2' new OTT release update: Unmuted international cut of Ranveer Singh film to release

Key facts

  • Unmuted international cut of Dhurandhar 2 coming to OTT
  • Stars Ranveer Singh
  • Theatrical India release had muted dialogue portions
  • International cut carries complete, unaltered audio

The Times of India reports that an 'unmuted' international cut of Ranveer Singh's Dhurandhar 2 is set to release on OTT. The theatrical release of the film in India featured portions of dialogue that were muted, likely as a condition of its certification by India's Central Board of Film Certification, while the version released internationally carried the complete audio.

The OTT release of this international cut means Indian viewers will be able to watch the film as originally intended by its makers, without the audio cuts that were present in the theatrical print. Such discrepancies between censored domestic prints and international versions have been a recurring point of discussion among Indian film audiences in recent years.

Dhurandhar 2 featuring Ranveer Singh generated significant buzz at the box office. The availability of the unmuted cut on OTT adds fresh momentum to its post-theatrical life, and is expected to draw viewers who either missed it in cinemas or are keen to see the uncensored version.
