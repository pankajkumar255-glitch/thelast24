Perizaad Zorabian: From Amitabh Bachchan's co-star to running a ₹120 crore poultry empire

Key facts

  • Perizaad Zorabian co-starred with Amitabh Bachchan in Bollywood
  • Left the film industry after marriage
  • Now runs Zorabian poultry business worth ₹120 crore

Perizaad Zorabian, once a sought-after Bollywood actress who starred alongside Amitabh Bachchan, left the film industry after marriage and channelled her energies into the family poultry business — which she has since grown into a ₹120 crore operation.

Zorabian was a recognisable presence in Hindi cinema in the late 1990s and early 2000s, known for her distinctive looks and performances in films alongside top-tier stars. Her exit from films was quiet, a personal choice tied to marriage, but what came next was anything but low-key.

The Zorabian brand, based in Mumbai, is today one of the better-known names in the processed poultry segment. Under her stewardship, the business scaled significantly, reaching revenues that would be the envy of many corporate ventures. The company supplies chicken products across Mumbai and has built a reputation for quality in a competitive market.

Her story cuts against the familiar narrative of Bollywood actresses fading into obscurity after leaving the industry. Instead, Perizaad Zorabian represents a rare archetype — the entertainer turned entrepreneur who built something measurable and lasting on her own terms.
