Akshay Kumar's Bhooth Bangla and More: Key OTT Releases This Week (June 8–14)

What happened

The Times of India lists the major OTT releases for the week of June 8 to 14, with Akshay Kumar's Bhooth Bangla as the lead Indian title. International offerings include Jason Statham's Shelter and the returning American series Sweet Magnolias, rounding out a varied week for streaming audiences.

The context

Akshay Kumar has been one of Bollywood's most prolific stars in recent years, with several films releasing directly on OTT platforms or transitioning to streaming shortly after theatrical runs. Jason Statham remains one of the most popular Hollywood action stars among Indian audiences.

Why it matters

A strong weekly OTT lineup directly shapes how tens of millions of Indian subscribers spend their leisure time and which platforms see subscriber engagement spikes. Akshay Kumar releases consistently draw large viewership numbers on Indian streaming services.

What's next

Expect viewership numbers and social media trends for Bhooth Bangla to emerge within days of its release, setting the tone for its commercial performance.
