'Small-caps represent the best opportunity in India right now'

What happened

Rediff reports that analysts are positioning Indian small-cap stocks as the best current investment opportunity in India. The assessment points to relatively attractive valuations after a period of underperformance compared to large-cap indices.

The context

Small-cap stocks in India had a strong run in 2023-24 but faced a sharp correction in 2024-25 amid regulatory caution from SEBI and concerns about frothy valuations. Millions of Indian retail investors hold small-cap exposure through dedicated mutual fund schemes. As markets stabilise and earnings visibility improves, fund managers periodically revisit the small-cap thesis.

Why it matters

Small-cap mutual funds are among the most widely held investment products by India's growing retail investor base; a sustained rally here could meaningfully boost household wealth and retirement savings for millions. Conversely, volatility in small-caps can also lead to sharp drawdowns.

What's next

Expect mutual fund houses to report inflows into small-cap schemes if the broader Nifty sustains above 24,000, as retail sentiment typically follows index momentum.
