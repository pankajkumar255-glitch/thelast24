Nitish Kumar Reddy sits out India's 2nd ODI against Afghanistan — here's why

Key facts

  • Nitish Kumar Reddy absent from India's 2nd ODI vs Afghanistan
  • Absence linked to fitness or workload management
  • Reddy is a key all-round option in India's ODI plans

The Times of India reports that Nitish Kumar Reddy has not been included in India's playing XI for the second ODI against Afghanistan, with his absence attributed to fitness or workload management reasons. Reddy had caught the eye of Indian cricket fans with his all-round performances and was considered a key part of India's evolving ODI setup.

Reddy's emergence as a genuine all-rounder gave India greater flexibility in team composition, and his absence forces the selectors and team management to reshuffle the batting order and bowling options. India's ongoing ODI series against Afghanistan is being used to experiment with combinations ahead of future assignments.

For fans following Indian cricket, Reddy's fitness status is important because all-rounders who can contribute with both bat and ball are rare and valuable commodities — his availability for upcoming matches will be closely watched.
