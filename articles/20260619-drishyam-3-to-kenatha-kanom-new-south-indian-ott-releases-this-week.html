Drishyam 3 to Kenatha Kanom: New South Indian OTT Releases This Week

Key facts

  • Drishyam 3 now streaming on OTT
  • Kenatha Kanom among other new releases
  • Covers Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada titles
  • Multiple platforms featured in the new release slate

The Economic Times reports a strong week for South Indian content on OTT, with Drishyam 3 headlining new Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada releases alongside films and shows including Kenatha Kanom. The roundup covers multiple platforms and represents one of the busier weeks for regional language streaming content.

Drishyam 3 is the most high-profile arrival, being the third entry in the Mohanlal-led Malayalam thriller franchise that has been a cultural phenomenon in India and spawned remakes in multiple languages. The original Drishyam and its sequel were among the biggest Malayalam films of their respective eras, and the third instalment carries considerable audience anticipation.

For everyday viewers across India — particularly those who follow Malayalam and South Indian cinema — the week's OTT slate offers significant new watching options without requiring a trip to the cinema, reinforcing how regional language content has become a primary driver of streaming platform engagement in India.
