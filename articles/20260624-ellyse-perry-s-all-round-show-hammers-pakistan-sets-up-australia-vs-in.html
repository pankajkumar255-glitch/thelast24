Ellyse Perry's all-round show hammers Pakistan, sets up Australia vs India Women's clash

Key facts

  • Ellyse Perry starred with an all-round performance against Pakistan
  • Australia beat Pakistan to set up a match against India
  • Perry's form a concern for India ahead of the fixture

Ellyse Perry underscored her status as one of women's cricket's finest all-rounders with a dominant display against Pakistan, helping Australia to a comprehensive victory that sets up a highly anticipated meeting with India. Perry's contributions across both disciplines of the game were central to Australia's commanding win.

The result eliminates Pakistan as a threat and means Australia arrive at the India fixture brimming with confidence. For India, the knowledge that Perry is in fine form will be a concern — she has been a consistent match-winner against them over the years and represents arguably the most dangerous individual player they will face.

Australia have long been the benchmark in women's cricket and their clinical performance against Pakistan reinforced that status. The manner of the win, with Perry leading the way, suggests the defending champions are peaking at the right moment of the tournament.

The forthcoming India-Australia tie is shaping up to be one of the tournament's standout fixtures. India will need their own all-round performances and sharp plans for Perry if they are to prevent Australia from continuing their march through the competition.
