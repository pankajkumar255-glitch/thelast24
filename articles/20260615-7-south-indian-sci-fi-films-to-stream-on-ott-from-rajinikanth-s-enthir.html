7 South Indian sci-fi films to stream on OTT: From Rajinikanth's Enthiran to Gaganachari

What happened

Pinkvilla has published a guide to seven South Indian science fiction films available on OTT platforms, headlined by Rajinikanth's Enthiran, the Telugu time-travel film Aditya 369, and the Malayalam film Gaganachari. The list draws attention to the genre's strong roots in Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam cinema.

The context

South Indian cinema, particularly Tamil and Telugu films, has produced several ambitious science fiction films that have outpaced Bollywood in terms of scale and concept. Enthiran, directed by S. Shankar and featuring Rajinikanth, remains one of the highest-grossing Indian films ever made and was a landmark moment for Indian sci-fi. The OTT boom has made these films accessible to pan-India and global audiences far beyond their original theatrical release windows.

Why it matters

OTT platforms have democratised access to regional language cinema for Indian audiences, and curated lists like this drive subscriptions and viewing hours while giving South Indian films a second life with new generations of viewers.

What's next

Expect streaming platforms to continue investing in and promoting South Indian sci-fi and genre content as audience data shows growing pan-India appetite for regional cinema.
