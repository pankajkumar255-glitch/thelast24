What's streaming this week: New OTT releases on Netflix, ZEE5, Prime Video, JioHotstar and Apple TV+

Key facts

  • Covers releases on Netflix, ZEE5, Prime Video, JioHotstar, Apple TV+
  • Week of June 15–21
  • Spans films, series across languages and genres

The Economic Times has published its weekly roundup of new OTT releases for June 15–21, covering all major streaming platforms available to Indian audiences — Netflix, ZEE5, Prime Video, JioHotstar, and Apple TV+. The guide consolidates the week's fresh arrivals in one place, spanning films, series, and other content across multiple languages and genres.

The Indian streaming market has grown intensely competitive, with platforms now racing to drop notable titles every week to retain subscribers. Weekly release guides have become a staple read for audiences navigating multiple subscriptions and trying to prioritise viewing.

For the average Indian household juggling two or more streaming subscriptions, knowing what is new — and on which platform — directly informs how people spend both their leisure time and their subscription budgets in any given week.
