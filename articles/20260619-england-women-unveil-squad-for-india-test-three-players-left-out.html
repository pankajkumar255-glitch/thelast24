England Women unveil squad for India Test, three players left out

Key facts

  • England Women named squad for Test match against India
  • Three players left out of England's Test squad
  • Match is a marquee women's Test fixture between top nations

The Times of India reports that England Women have unveiled their squad for the Test match against India, with the notable exclusion of three players from the group. The squad announcement sets the stage for what promises to be a high-profile red-ball encounter between two of women's cricket's top nations.

The three omissions are the talking point around England's selection, with the dropped players' absence likely to influence the balance and experience within England's line-up. Women's Test cricket between India and England has historically attracted significant attention, with the format enjoying a revival in recent years as cricket boards commit to scheduling more red-ball fixtures for women.

For Indian fans, the match represents a rare opportunity to watch their women's team compete in the longest format of the game. England's squad composition will give India's coaching staff and selectors valuable intelligence about the opposition's plans as both teams prepare for a Test that carries considerable prestige in the women's game.
