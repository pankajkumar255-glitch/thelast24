Drishyam 3, Athiradi and more South Indian films arrive on OTT this week

Key facts

  • Drishyam 3 leads South Indian OTT releases this week
  • Athiradi also among new arrivals
  • Films span Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu and Kannada languages

What happened

Moneycontrol reports that Drishyam 3 headlines the South Indian OTT releases of the week, joined by Athiradi and other new films across Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada languages. The report lists these as the key digital premieres available to streaming audiences this week.

The context

The Drishyam franchise, starring Mohanlal and directed by Jeethu Joseph, is one of the most successful thriller series in Indian cinema, with the first two films remade in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and other languages. Drishyam 2 was a massive theatrical and OTT success, making the third installment highly anticipated. South Indian content has consistently ranked among the most-watched material on Indian OTT platforms in recent years.

Why it matters

Drishyam 3's digital arrival gives streaming services a significant content event likely to spike subscriptions and viewership, while also signalling that premium South Indian theatrical hits are reaching OTT within a relatively short window.

What's next

Expect Drishyam 3's OTT viewership numbers to be closely watched as a benchmark for Malayalam cinema's streaming pull ahead of future franchise instalments.
