PCB Chief Mohsin Naqvi Plans Radical Overhaul of Pakistan Cricket Amid Prolonged Slump

What happened

India Today reports that PCB chairman Mohsin Naqvi is preparing to bring radical changes to the Pakistan Cricket Board's structure and team management, driven by Pakistan cricket's recent struggles in international competitions.

The context

Pakistan cricket has faced significant turbulence in recent years, including early exits from major tournaments, captaincy changes, and internal disputes. The PCB has seen frequent leadership and policy shifts, and Mohsin Naqvi's tenure as chairman has already been marked by controversy and calls for accountability from fans and former players.

Why it matters

A structural overhaul of the PCB could reset Pakistan's cricketing direction ahead of major upcoming ICC events, which in turn affects India's tournament rivals and the competitive landscape of world cricket.

What's next

Expect official announcements from the PCB on specific changes to team management and administrative structures in the coming weeks.
