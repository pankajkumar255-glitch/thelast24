Irfan Pathan advises Rishabh Pant to draw inspiration from a senior India star

What happened

NDTV Sports reports that former India cricketer Irfan Pathan has advised Rishabh Pant to take inspiration from a particular senior India star to improve and stabilise his game. The report covers Pathan's public comments, which appear to be directed at helping Pant navigate a phase of inconsistency or adjustment in his international career. The specific Indian star Pathan cited is named in the original report. Pant has been one of India's key players since his return from injury.

The context

Rishabh Pant suffered a near-fatal road accident in December 2022 and made a remarkable return to competitive cricket, playing for India across all three formats. He is widely regarded as one of India's most match-winning and unconventional batters, capable of single-handedly changing the course of a game. However, questions about his consistency and shot selection have surfaced at times. Irfan Pathan, a respected voice in Indian cricket media, regularly offers public analysis and advice on player development.

Why it matters

Pant's form is central to India's batting plans in Tests and white-ball cricket, so any public discussion of his development and influences is closely followed by fans and selectors alike. Guidance from experienced former players like Pathan can reflect broader discussions happening within the cricketing establishment. For fans, it is a window into how India's think-tank views one of their most exciting but unpredictable match-winners.

What's next

Expect Pant's performances in upcoming series, including the Afghanistan ODIs and the Ireland T20Is, to be scrutinised through the lens of his consistency and maturity as a batter. His response to such public advice will likely be assessed in how he approaches his innings in the coming weeks.
