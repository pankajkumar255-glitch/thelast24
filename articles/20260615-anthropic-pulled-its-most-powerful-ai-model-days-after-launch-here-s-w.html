Anthropic Pulled Its Most Powerful AI Model Days After Launch — Here's Why

What happened

NDTV reports that Anthropic disabled its most powerful AI model within days of its launch, citing US government restrictions. Mint reports that Indian AI startups are now confronting new risks following Anthropic's withdrawal of Fable 5 from accessible markets. Business Standard notes the curbs have reignited calls for India to pursue sovereign AI development.

The context

Anthropic, the US-based AI safety company backed by Amazon and Google, is among the world's leading large language model developers. The US government has increasingly moved to restrict exports of advanced AI models to certain countries, framing it as a national security measure. Indian startups have been among the most active adopters of frontier AI APIs from US companies.

Why it matters

Indian businesses that built products on Anthropic's most capable model now face potential service interruptions, forcing costly pivots to alternative platforms. The episode underscores India's exposure to geopolitical decisions made in Washington that can instantly affect domestic technology ecosystems.

What's next

Expect the Indian government and industry bodies to accelerate discussions around funding and deploying domestically built foundational AI models to reduce reliance on foreign platforms.
