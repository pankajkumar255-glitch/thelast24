Nike India Leases 1.25 Lakh Sq Ft Bengaluru Office at Rs 121 Crore for Five Years

What happened

Hindustan Times reports that Nike India has leased 1.25 lakh square feet of office space in Bengaluru for a period of five years, with the total rent amounting to Rs 121 crore. The deal reflects Nike's expanding India footprint, particularly in technology and digital operations.

The context

Bengaluru is India's largest commercial real estate market for Grade-A office space and has attracted major global technology and retail firms including Google, Microsoft, and Amazon. Nike has been growing its India technology centre, which supports global digital, supply chain, and analytics operations. Commercial office leasing in Bengaluru has remained robust despite global work-from-home trends.

Why it matters

A Rs 121 crore lease commitment by Nike will create or sustain hundreds of high-paying jobs in Bengaluru and reinforces the city's premium office market, keeping rental rates elevated across the tech corridor.

What's next

Expect Nike to announce hiring plans and the specific functions — likely technology, data analytics, and e-commerce — that will be housed in the new Bengaluru office.
