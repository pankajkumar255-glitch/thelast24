Privacy policy.

Short version: we collect as little as possible, and nothing without telling you.

What we collect

  • Newsletter signups: your email address and the topic preferences you select. Used only to send you the weekly digest you asked for. Unsubscribe anytime via the link in any email or by replying.
  • Cookies and local storage: we store your cookie choice, whether you've dismissed the newsletter prompt, and — if you consent — analytics identifiers. Choosing "Essential only" limits us to functional storage.
  • Analytics (with consent): aggregate readership data such as page views and approximate region. No sale of personal data, ever.

Advertising

This site displays advertising through Google AdSense. Google and its partners use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting aboutads.info. Where required, ads are personalised only with your consent, which you give through the cookie banner; choosing "Essential only" limits ad personalisation.

For more on how Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps, see Google's policy.

Third parties we rely on

  • Hosting and deployment infrastructure (e.g. GitHub Pages / Vercel)
  • Email delivery for the newsletter (the provider you signed up through)
  • Photo licensing via Pexels; original reporting belongs to the linked publishers

Your rights

You can request deletion of your newsletter data anytime via contact. You can change your cookie choice by clearing this site's data in your browser.

We comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act. If you have questions about how your data is handled, contact us anytime.
