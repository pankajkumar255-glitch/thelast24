Homemakers are 'nation builders', their work worth at least Rs 30,000 a month: Supreme Court

The ruling is significant in the context of compensation claims — particularly in accident and insurance cases — where courts have historically undervalued or ignored the economic contribution of women who work at home. The judgment marks a shift in how Indian law formally reckons with unpaid domestic work.
